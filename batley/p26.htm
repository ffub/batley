Descendants of Joseph Hall
     George Hall was born on 21 January 1798. He was the son of Joseph Hall and Elizabeth Taylor. George Hall was baptized on 18 February 1798 at Batley Parish Church.1
He married Mary Willans on 17 September 1818 at Batley witnesses John Newsome and Joseph Chadwick.1
George Hall was appears to have moved to Dewsbury sometime between the birth of Emma in 1838 and Susan Phoebe in 1839. At the baptism of the latter he is described as of Dewsbury, formerly of Batley.1
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at Aldams House, Dewsbury, as a woollen "M" -presumably manufacturer. At Abraham's baptism earlier that year he was described as merchant and manufacturer.2
1851 George Hall and son were listed as woollen manufacturers and also carpet and rug manufacturers at Aldams Mill, Dewsbury.3,4
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Aldams Head, Town End, Dewsbury, as a clothier aged 53. Also present is a grandaughter, Louisa Ann Hall, aged 2.5
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at 12 Knowl,, Mirfield, as a woollen manufacturer, a widower aged 63.6
