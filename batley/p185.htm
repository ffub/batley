John Frost Burnley

#5340, b. 1841, d. 27 May 1895
John Frost Burnley|b. 1841\nd. 27 May 1895|p185.htm#i5340|Abraham Burnley|b. 1817|p68.htm#i2049|Betty Roberts|b. c 1820|p185.htm#i5339|Samuel Burnley|b. 27 Mar 1789|p68.htm#i2046|Elizabeth Newsome|b. 14 Feb 1794|p61.htm#i1848|||||||

© image collection V E Tomlinson
     John Frost Burnley was born in 1841 at Batley. He was the son of Abraham Burnley and Betty Roberts. John Frost Burnley married Sarah Farrar, daughter of Joseph Farrar, in 1865.1,2
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at Bank Foot, Batley, as a cloth dresser.3
In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as the head of household at Talbot Street, Batley, as a foreman cloth finisher.4
In the census of 5 April 1891 he was listed as the head of household at 4 Wilsons Buildings, Bradford Road, Batley, as a widower, a woollen manufacturer.5
A partnership with Arthur Wilson as woolllen cloth manufacturers was dissolved in 1892.6
John Frost Burnley died on 27 May 1895 at Batley.7

Child of John Frost Burnley and Sarah Farrar

  • Joseph Burnley b. c 1867
