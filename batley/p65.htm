She married Benjamin Farrar, son of Joseph Farrar and Ruth Drake, on 26 December 1824 at Batley.1
She was listed in the census of 30 March 1851 at 26 J Ward's Hill, Batley, bobbin winder.2
In the census of 7 April 1861 she was listed as the head of household at Ben Sheard's Fold, John Ward's Hill, Batley, aged 56.3
Hannah Maria Sheard died on 6 August 1864 at age 59. She was buried on 8 August 1864 at Batley.1
