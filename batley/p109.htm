     George Sheard Fox was born on 23 November 1843.1 He was the son of David Fox and Margaret Sheard. George Sheard Fox was baptized on 10 March 1844 at Batley.1
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as a scholar the son of David Fox at 62 Great George Street, Bermondsey, London.2
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as a Shoddy manufacturer's clerk the brother of Hortensi Fox.3
George Sheard Fox married Nanny Senior in 1866.4
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at Caledonia Road, Batley, Clerk to mungo manufacturer.5
He was listed in the census of 5 April 1891 as a cashier and book keeper.6
George Sheard Fox is likely to have been the Mr Sheard Fox who, along with H. D. Smith led the office staff and foremen who acted as pall bearers at the funeral of  John Blackburn in 1900.7
George Sheard Fox lived at Soothill Hall in 1900.

Children of George Sheard Fox and Nanny Senior

  • Margaret Ann Fox b. 12 Sep 1867
  • Ernest Edward Fox b. 30 Nov 1868
  • Charles Herbert Fox b. 17 Jul 1870
  • Senior Frederick L Fox b. 7 Apr 1872
  • Maud Mary Fox b. 8 Jan 1874
