     Thomas Hudswell was born circa 1770, probably the son of Grace Hudswell baptised at Batley December 7th 1770.
For him to be Thomas Taylor's cousin, as stated by T.C, Taylor, Grace must have been a daughter of Joshua Hudswell, as was Thomas Taylor's mother.

Thomas Hudswell was one of the Company who first worked Clerk Green Mill together with George Ineson and Barnabas Ineson.
