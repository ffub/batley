Relationship=3rd cousin 4 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     Alfred Parker was born on 20 February 1841 at Batley. He was the son of Joseph Parker and Jane Blakeley. Alfred Parker was baptized on 4 April 1841 at Zion Chapel, Batley.
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as aged 3 months the son of Joseph Parker at Providence Place, Batley.1
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as a scholar the son of Joseph Parker at Providence Street, Batley.2
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as a rag dealer the son of Joseph Parker at Providence Street, Batley.3
Alfred Parker married Emma Sheard, daughter of Joseph Sheard and Sarah Ann Colbeck, on 11 January 1865 at Batley.4,5
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at Lower Soothill as a woollen manufacturer.6
In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as the head of household at Albert Terrace, Batley, as a woollen manufacturer.7
Alfred Parker died on 29 July 1898 at Toronto at age 57.
