Mary Wilson

#3096, b. 25 February 1809, d. 7 October 1889
Mary Wilson|b. 25 Feb 1809\nd. 7 Oct 1889|p104.htm#i3096|Daniel Wilson|b. 13 Nov 1785\nd. 24 Jan 1857|p108.htm#i3224|Letitia Fox|b. 4 Oct 1784\nd. 1 Nov 1852|p121.htm#i3587|Daniel Wilson||p108.htm#i3210||||Joseph Fox|b. 1751\nd. 1832|p121.htm#i3589|Mary Senior|b. 28 Oct 1753|p88.htm#i2659|
     Mary Wilson was born on 25 February 1809. She was the daughter of Daniel Wilson and Letitia Fox. Mary Wilson was baptized on 2 April 1809 at Batley.1
She married George Sheard, son of Michael Sheard and Sarah Lister, on 25 July 1831 at Batley He wrote his name, she made her mark. Witnesses were Jeremiah Chadwick and Joseph Clarkson.1
Mary Sheard was listed on the census of 6 June 1841 at Hick Lane, Batley, as wife of George Sheard.2
In the census of 30 March 1851 Mary Sheard was listed at Commercial St. North side, Batley, as wife of George Sheard.3
In the census of 7 April 1861 she was listed as the head of household at Hick Lane, Batley, as a widow aged 52, a proprietor of houses.4
Mary Sheard married Abraham Blakeley, son of John Blakeley and Mary Blackburn, in 1861.5
Mary's identification as daughter of Daniel based on her baptism corresponding to her age in 1851 and 1861, but later appears confirmed by identities of a nephew and niece living with her in 1861.
In the census of 2 April 1871 Mary Blakeley was listed at Hick Lane, Batley, as wife of Abraham Blakeley.6
In the census of 3 April 1881 she was listed as aged 74, the aunt of John Thomas Hepworth at Field Hill, Batley.7
Mary Blakeley died on 7 October 1889 at age 80.8
Her estate was probated to Benjamin Sheard, Batley, Woollen manufacturer, nephew.8

     Percy William Longbottom Sheard was born on 25 December 1871 at Batley.1 He was the son of James Sheard and Hannah Nicholson. Percy William Longbottom Sheard was baptized on 5 July 1874 at Batley.1
In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as a scholar the son of James Sheard at Commercial St, Batley.2
In the census of 5 April 1891 he was listed as ironmongers apprentice the son of Hannah Sheard at 40 Commercial St, Batley.3
Percy William Longbottom Sheard died on 13 April 1917 at France at age 45.
