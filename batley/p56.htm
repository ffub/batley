In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at Batley Carr Road, Dewsbury, as a rag merchant. there are two domestic servants, May Pearce and Hannah Burton.2

In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at Spring Field Terrace, Dewsbury, as a widower, a woollen rag merchant.4
In 1864 Robert Blakeley and Sons were listed as manufacturers of English and Importers of foreign shoddy and mungo, at Bradford Road, Dewsbury.5
He died on 8 March 1869 at age 64.
