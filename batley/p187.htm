#5387, b. 12 March 1810

Relationship=3rd cousin 5 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     Mary Ann Willans was born on 12 March 1810 at Batley.1 She was the daughter of John Willans and Mary King.1 Mary Ann Willans was baptized on 17 September 1810 at Batley.1
She married Benjamin Blakeley, son of Samuel Blakeley and Mary Wharton, on 27 October 1833 at Batley witnesses David Blakeley and Harriet Blakeley.1
In the census of 6 June 1841 Mary Ann Blakeley was listed at Upper Batley, Batley, as wife of Benjamin Blakeley.2
In the census of 30 March 1851 Mary Ann Blakeley was listed at Bracken Hill, Mirfield, as wife of Benjamin Blakeley.3
In the census of 7 April 1861 Mary Ann Blakeley was listed at Carlinghow, Batley, as wife of Benjamin Blakeley.4

Children of Mary Ann Willans and Benjamin Blakeley

  • Christopher Blakeley b. c 1835
  • John Willans Blakeley b. 1838
  • Eliza Blakeley b. c Mar 1840
  • Benjamin Blakeley b. c 1844
  • Thomas Blakeley b. c 1846
  • Mary Blakeley b. c 1848
  • James Blakeley+ b. 1850
