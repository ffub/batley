Relationship=4th cousin 3 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     Joshua Colbeck was born circa 1866 at Batley. He was the son of William Colbeck and Sarah Burnley. In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as a scholar aged 13 the son of William Colbeck at Wellington Street, Batley.1
Joshua Colbeck was buried on 5 June 1937 at The cemetery, Batley, ; aged 71.2
