Florence Allen

#6899, b. 1881
© image courtesy of Clive Mason
     Florence Allen was born in 1881. She married Reginald Sheard, son of John Sheard and Margaret Maria Hodgson Hogg, in 1902 at Leeds.1
In the census of 2 April 1911 Florence Sheard was listed at 13 Bewdley Hill, Kidderminster, Worcestershire, as wife of Reginald Sheard.2
Reginald having died in 1914, Florence Sheard married Joseph W Radcliff in 1919.3

Children of Florence Allen and Reginald Sheard

  • Florence Margaret Sheard b. 1908
  • Rosemary Florence Sheard b. 1913
