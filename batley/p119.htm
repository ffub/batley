     Hiram Preston was born in 1839 at Batley. He was the son of John Preston and Hannah Firth. Hiram Preston was baptized on 7 April 1839 at Dewsbury.
Hiram Preston was also known as Iram Preston (with dropped aitch in Yorkshire fashion). In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the son of John Preston at Batley New mill, Batley.1
Hiram Preston married Sarah Ann Blackburn, daughter of William Blackburn and Sarah Thompson, on 15 April 1865 at Batley Witnesses were Joseph Preston and George Blackburn. Sarah Ann, who made her mark, gave her father William's occupation as manufacturer.2,3
Hiram Preston married Ann Kirby in 1870.4
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household as a Foreman in a rag warehouse.5
In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as the head of household at No 10 St. George Square, Batley, a foreman in rag sorting.6
Hiram Preston died in 1896.7

Children of Hiram Preston and Ann Kirby

  • Sarah Ann Preston b. 12 Jan 1870
  • Ada Preston b. c 1872
