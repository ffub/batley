Roberta Parker

#8167, b. circa 1841
     Roberta Parker was born circa 1841 at Heckmondwike. She was listed in the census of 7 April 1861 as a house servant in the in the household of George Sheard at Staincliffe Hill, Batley.1
By this time there are two small children in the household; By this time, with two small children and Annie pregnant again a housemaid and a nurse. Roberta Parker married John Crabtree in 1865.2

#8180, b. circa 1868
