James Talbot Wailes

#5109, b. 1847
James Talbot Wailes|b. 1847|p176.htm#i5109|Noah Wailes|b. c 1825|p111.htm#i3295|Hannah Talbot|b. 1825|p17.htm#i518|||||||James Talbot|b. 3 Apr 1800\nd. 19 Jul 1829|p17.htm#i511|Hannah Newsome|b. 6 Jun 1803\nd. 24 May 1830|p17.htm#i510|

Relationship=4th cousin 3 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     James Talbot Wailes was born in 1847. He was the son of Noah Wailes and Hannah Talbot. James Talbot Wailes was baptized on 19 September 1847 at Batley as son of Noah and Hannah Talbot. Noah listed as an engineer.1
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as a scholar the son of Noah Wailes at Havercoft, Batley.2
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as a rag sorter the son of Noah Wailes at Commercial St, Batley.3
James Talbot Wailes married Eliza Spark on 15 February 1869 at Scarborough.4,5
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at Talbot Street, Batley, as a rag merchant. Also present is Frances Wilson, a farmer's wife, also from Scarborough like James's wife, and I think this reads sister, presumably of Eliza.6
