Descendants of Joseph Hall
     Joseph Hall was born in 1815. He was the son of John Hall and Sarah Gledhill. Joseph Hall was baptized on 26 March 1815 at Batley.1
He married Ann Driver on 23 April 1835 at Batley.1
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at New Batley as a rag grinder.2
In the census of 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Clerk Green, Batley, as a rag dealer.3
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at New Street, Batley, clothier, woollen weaver.4
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at New Street, Batley.5
In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as the head of household at New Street, Batley, as a rag grinder, woollen.6

Children of Joseph Hall and Ann Driver

  • John Hall b. c 1837
  • Joseph Hall b. 3 Jul 1840
  • George Driver Hall b. 8 Oct 1842, d. 1848
  • James Hall b. 21 Nov 1844
  • Charles Hall b. 4 Dec 1847
  • Sarah Hall b. 29 Dec 1849
  • Hartley Hall b. c 1856

In the census of 2 April 1871 she was listed as the head of household at Purlwell Lane, Batley, as a widow aged 69.4
