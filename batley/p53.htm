Relationship=1st cousin 5 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     Elizabeth Stubley was born on 13 April 1786.1 She was the daughter of David Stubley and Ann Colbeck. Elizabeth Stubley was baptized on 28 May 1786 at Batley.1
She married Samuel Blakeley, son of William Blakeley and Hannah Newsome, on 4 November 1805.
In the census of 6 June 1841 Elizabeth Blakeley was listed at Havercroft, Batley, as wife of Samuel Blakeley.2
She was listed as a widower, a Woollen Flock Merchant in the census of 30 March 1851 living with her son William Blakeley at J Ward's Hill, Batley.3 Elizabeth Stubley died on 15 August 1865 at age 79.4
