Arthur Kaye

#7784, b. 1882
Arthur Kaye|b. 1882|p279.htm#i7784|George Kaye|b. 1855|p279.htm#i7782|||||||Ellen Blackburn|b. 1831|p279.htm#i7780|||||||
     Arthur Kaye was born in 1882. He was the son of George Kaye. Arthur Kaye married Annie Eveline Senior, daughter of John Hall Senior and Elizabeth (?), in 1911.
Arthur and Annie Eveline Kaye with their children emigrated in 1914 on the S S "Cedric", arriving in New York on the 2nd October.1 In the census of 9 January 1920 he was listed as the head of household at Willow St, Little Falls, Herkimer, New York, as foreman, packing room, knitting mill.2
Alan Stewart-Kaye's interesting account of how he discovered what had become of his relative Arthur appeared in the January 2011 Journal of the Huddersfield & District Family History Society.

Children of Arthur Kaye and Annie Eveline Senior

  • John George Kaye3 b. 1912
  • Squire Senior Kaye b. 1914
