John Burnley

#2654, b. 8 October 1803, d. 1860
John Burnley|b. 8 Oct 1803\nd. 1860|p88.htm#i2654|Christopher Burnley|b. 27 Jun 1768\nd. 23 Sep 1849|p142.htm#i4231|Mary Brook||p142.htm#i4232||||Grace Burnley||p197.htm#i5662|Richard Brook||p197.htm#i5663||||
     John Burnley was born on 8 October 1803 at Batley.1 He was the son of Christopher Burnley and Mary Brook. John Burnley was baptized on 11 December 1803 at Batley.1
He married Ann Stubley, daughter of Joshua Stubley and Mary Blackburn, on 27 March 1826 at Batley witnesses J Chadwick and Joseph Webster.1
He was listed in the census of 1841 at New Batley, Batley, as a clothier.2
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at New Street, Batley, as a woollen rag merchant.3
On 20 August 1852 John Burnley married Mary Stubley, daughter of Thomas Redfearn and Mary Hall, at Batley Mary was his first wife's brother's widow. John Stubley had died the previous year. Witnesses were Jeremiah Redfearn and Margaret Donnegan.4
John Burnley died in 1860. He was buried on 28 January 1860 at Batley.1

Children of John Burnley and Ann Stubley

  • Christopher Burnley+ b. 1827

     Isaac Sheard was born in 1823. He was the son of David Sheard and Martha Talbot. Isaac Sheard was baptized on 6 April 1823 at Batley.1
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at Havercroft as a woollen weaver aged 18, with Harriet, 20.2
He married Harriet Stubley, daughter of George Stubley and Hannah Lister, in 1845 at St Peters, Leeds.3,4
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at 16 J Ward's Hill, Batley, as a cloth weaver.5
In 1855 Isaac Sheard received a legacy of £50 in will of his uncle George Sheard.
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at Back Street, Mirfield, as a woollen miller.
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at Back Street, Mirfield, as a cloth miller. He is shown here with a wife Jane, but I have not been able to find a likely second marriage.6
He was probably the one who married Sarah Sykes on 10 November 1873 at Birstall.7,8
In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as the head of household at Marriott Street, Dewsbury, as a cloth fuller.
Isaac Sheard appeared on the census of 5 April 1891 in the household of Robert Hudson and Abigail Hudson at Scout Hill, Dewsbury,as a widower aged 68.9 Isaac Sheard died in 1895; aged 72.10

     Elizabeth Chadwick Taylor was born on 9 October 1853.1 She was the daughter of John Taylor and Sarah Chadwick. In the census of 7 April 1861 she was listed as a scholar the daughter of John Taylor at Cross Bank, Batley.2
In the census of 2 April 1871 she was listed as the daughter of John Taylor at Ramsill House,, Upper Batley.3
Elizabeth Chadwick Taylor married James Joshua Carter, son of Joshua Carter and Ann (?), in 1883.4
In the census of 5 April 1891 Elizabeth Chadwick Taylor was listed at Upper Batley as wife of James Joshua Carter.5
Elizabeth Chadwick Taylor died on 11 July 1918 at age 64.1

Children of Elizabeth Chadwick Taylor and James Joshua Carter

  • Eleanor S Carter b. c 1884
  • John Carter b. c 1885

Relationship=3rd cousin 4 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     Thomas Blakeley was born on 30 December 1826. He was the son of William Blakeley and Sarah Hargreaves. Thomas Blakeley was born circa 1827 at Batley. He was baptized on 21 January 1836 at Zion Chapel ( listed as 1837), Batley.
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as as a woollen labourer the son of William Blakeley at Silver St, Batley.1
Thomas Blakeley married Elizabeth Blakeley Burnley, daughter of John Burnley and Harriet Fox, in 1849.2
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Low Soothill as a woollen spinner.3
Thomas Blakeley made a will signed on 18 April 1864 which mentioned wife Elizabeth, brother David Blakeley and wife's brother John Burnley.4
He died on 16 January 1865 at age 38.
