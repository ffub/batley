David Healey

#8455, b. 1827, d. 2 November 1872
David Healey|b. 1827\nd. 2 Nov 1872|p305.htm#i8455|William Healey|b. 13 Jun 1788|p305.htm#i8453|Mary Ann Hartley||p305.htm#i8454|John Healey|b. 1750|p198.htm#i5680|Esther Whitworth|b. 16 Sep 1753|p198.htm#i5681|||||||
     David Healey was born in 1827. He was the son of William Healey and Mary Ann Hartley. David Healey was baptized on 15 April 1827 at Batley.1
He married Susannah Brown on 4 July 1848 at Batley.1
He was listed in the census of 30 March 1851 at Providence Street, Batley, as a cloth spinner.2
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at Park Street, Batley, as a rag merchant employing 12 women. Also present is a nephew, Alfred Hepworth aged 8 and a general servant Elizabeth Brown, probably a relation of his wife Susanna.3
David Healey married Priscilla Duckworh on 17 October 1865 at Batley.1
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at Park road, Batley, as a woollen manufacturer. the family are now employing Charles Robinson, probably a connection of David's wife Priscilla, who was also from Barnoldswick and had earlier been employed by Charles's father. There are also two other servants.
David Healey died on 2 November 1872.4

Children of David Healey and Priscilla Duckworh

  • Ada Healey b. c 1867
  • William Henry Healey b. c 1869
  • Helena Healey b. c 1870
