Flora Annie Sykes1

#3423, b. 11 July 1861, d. 1 January 1897
Flora Annie Sykes|b. 11 Jul 1861\nd. 1 Jan 1897|p115.htm#i3423|Ingham Sykes|b. c 1815|p149.htm#i4422|Flora (?)|b. c 1830|p149.htm#i4423|||||||||||||
     Flora Annie Sykes was born on 11 July 1861 at Batley.2 She was the daughter of Ingham Sykes and Flora (?). Flora Annie Sykes was baptized on 9 February 1862 at Batley.2
She married William Blackburn, son of Thomas Blackburn and Elizabeth Terry, on 20 September 1883 at Batley William was described as an ovelooker.3
In the census of 5 April 1891 Flora Annie Blackburn was listed at 37 Bradford Road, Batley, as wife of William Blackburn.4
Flora Annie Blackburn died on 1 January 1897 at age 35.5,6

Children of Flora Annie Sykes and William Blackburn

  • Mark Ingham Blackburn b. 6 Sep 1885
  • Minnie Blackburn b. 14 Jun 1887
