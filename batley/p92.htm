Joseph Sheard

#2773, b. 1817, d. 14 February 1878
Joseph Sheard|b. 1817\nd. 14 Feb 1878|p92.htm#i2773||||Hannah Sheard|b. 4 Dec 1796\nd. 1834|p62.htm#i1876|||||||George Sheard|b. 1773\nd. 1835|p4.htm#i106|Ann Greenwood|b. 4 Jul 1775\nd. 1832|p43.htm#i1285|

Relationship=2nd cousin 4 times removed of Vivien Eley.
Charts
Descendants of Michael Sheard born 1688
      Joseph Sheard was also known as Joseph Roberts. He was born in 1817. He was the son of Hannah Sheard. Joseph Sheard was baptized on 3 November 1817 at Batley on the same day as his mother's marriage to Joseph Roberts.1

In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed in the household of James Sheard at Havercroft, Batley, the identification of the Joseph living with James as son of Hannah is based on the fact that James was Hannah's brother.2
Joseph Sheard married Martha Preston, daughter of George Preston, on 20 July 1845 at Batley witnesses were Michael Preston and Joseph Chadwick.1
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household as a coal miner aged 33.3
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household as a Coal Miner.4
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at High St, Batley, as a weighman at colliery.5
Joseph Sheard died on 14 February 1878; aged 60 years.6

In the census of 2 April 1871 Ann Sheard was listed as a power loom weaver at Inman Fold, Ravensthorpe, wife of Jacob Sheard.2
In the census of 3 April 1881 Ann Sheard was listed at Albion Street, Mirfield, as wife of Jacob Sheard.3
In 1883 Ann Sheard and Jacob Sheard arrived in Little Falls, NY having sailed on the Wisconsin with three children, Isaac, Florence and Alice, arriving in New York on the 19th June.4,5 Ann Sheard accompanied her husband's aunt Mary Seniorand Isabella Mitchell and Martha Clafton on a visit to England in 1889. The party left New York in June and returned in October, arriving on the 15th on the "Aurania."5 In the census of 1892 Ann Sheard was listed at Little Falls, Herkimer, New York, as wife of Jacob Sheard.6
In the census of 1900 Ann Sheard was listed at 48½ Furnace St, Little Falls, Herkimer, New York, as wife of Jacob Sheard.7
Ann Naylor died on 18 July 1901 at Little Falls, NY, at age 58.

Relationship=3rd great-aunt of Vivien Eley.
      Tabitha Senior was also known as Batthy Green. She was born circa 1807. She was the daughter of John Senior and Ann Haigh. Tabitha Senior married Charles Green on 1 October 1827 at High Hoyland.
In the census of 6 June 1841 Batthy Green was listed at Skelmanthorpe as wife of Charles Green.1
Tabitha Senior died in 1849. She was buried on 28 December 1849 at St Augustine, Scissett, ; aged 42.2
