William Harrison

#3567, b. 1821, d. 28 October 1883
William Harrison|b. 1821\nd. 28 Oct 1883|p120.htm#i3567|Samuel Harrison|b. c 1785\nd. Nov 1858|p23.htm#i700|Rebecca Hall|b. 26 Jan 1783|p23.htm#i686|||||||Thomas Hall|b. 1750\nd. 8 Sep 1835|p23.htm#i680|Frances Allott|b. 1749\nd. 25 Sep 1826|p23.htm#i681|

Relationship=1st cousin 5 times removed of Vivien Eley.
Charts
Descendants of Joseph Hall
image courtesy of Kirlees Image Archive
     William Harrison was born in 1821. He was the son of Samuel Harrison and Rebecca Hall. William Harrison was baptized on 22 April 1821 at Batley.1
He married Rebecca Morrell on 4 April 1844 at Spofforth.2,3

In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at New St, Batley, as in firm of 9 employing 30 men 3 women, 27 boys, 10 girls. & woollen manufacturer employing 5 men and 3 women.4
The former must refer to the partnership at Clerk Green Mill with members of the Brearley Family and other descendants of Thomas Hall.
He was listed in the census of 7 April 1861 at Spring Gardens, Batley, as a woollen manufacturer employing 10 men and women.5
In 1869 William Harrison was a member of the first Batley Town Council.
Rebecca had died in 1867, and by 1871 we find William remarried to Elizabeth (?).
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at Spring Gardens, Batley, as a woollen cloth manufacturer and Alderman.6
In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as the head of household at Townend, Ossett cum Gawthorpe, as surveyor to the Local Board.7
William Harrison died on 28 October 1883 at Spring Gardens, Batley; aged 62.8

Children of William Harrison and Rebecca Morrell

  • Samuel Morrell Harrison b. c 1844
  • Ann Mary Harrison+ b. c 1847
  • Henry Harrison b. c 1850
  • Edwin Harrison b. c 1852
  • Alfred Harrison b. c 1853
  • Hannah Frances Harrison b. c 1855
