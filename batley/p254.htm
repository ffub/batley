Elizabeth Hebert

#7152, b. May 1862, d. 1935
     Elizabeth Hebert was born in May 1862. She married Francis M Senior, son of Francis Senior and Mary Sheard, circa 1884.
In the census of 9 June 1900 Elizabeth Senior was listed at Little Falls, Herkimer, New York, as wife of Francis M Senior.1
In the census of 19 April 1910 Elizabeth Senior was listed at Little Falls, Herkimer, New York, as wife of Francis M Senior.2
In the census of 17 January 1920 Elizabeth Senior was listed at Little Falls, Herkimer, New York, as wife of Francis M Senior.3
In 1923 Elizabeth and Francis M Senior made a visit to England, sailing first class to Liverpool on the Adriatic, arriving on the 20th May, and returned on the Majestic, arrriving back in New York on the first of August on the Majestic.4,5
In the census of 8 April 1930 Elizabeth Senior was listed at Little Falls, Herkimer, New York, as wife of Francis M Senior.6
Elizabeth Hebert died in 1935.

Children of Elizabeth Hebert and Francis M Senior

  • Mary M Senior b. Jul 1886
  • James H Senior+ b. Apr 1893
  • Frank H Senior b. 1903
