     Mary Jane Wilson was born on 21 September 1869 at Falhouse, Briestfield.1 She was the daughter of Squire Wilson and Martha Sowerby. Her father was described as a coal miner at that time.
In the census of 5 April 1891 she was listed as a dress maker the daughter of Squire Wilson at Huddersfield Rd, Ravensthorpe.2
Mary Jane Wilson married Will Sheard, son of William Sheard and Emily Walker Fawcett, on 8 August 1896 at St Saviour's Church, Ravensthorpe.3
In the census of 31 March 1901 Mary Jane Sheard was listed at Syke Terrace, Hipperholme, as wife of Will Sheard.4

In the census of 2 April 1911 Mary Jane Sheard was listed at 27 Orchard Street, Savile Town, Dewsbury, as wife of Will Sheard.5
Mary Jane Sheard died on 5 April 1952 at age 82.6 She was buried in April 1952 at the Parish Churchyard, Mirfield.
