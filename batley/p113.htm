Abraham Talbot

#3352, b. 21 November 1828
Abraham Talbot|b. 21 Nov 1828|p113.htm#i3352|John Talbot|b. 24 Feb 1787\nd. 27 Mar 1853|p47.htm#i1384|Hannah Slater|b. c 1788\nd. 4 Oct 1846|p88.htm#i2678|John Talbot|b. 1753\nd. 1 May 1808|p48.htm#i1422|Hannah Carr|b. 9 Apr 1758\nd. 2 Aug 1840|p47.htm#i1387|William Slater||p90.htm#i2728||||
Abraham Talbot with son Charles Henry and grandson Charles Reginald. Collection of Vivien E Tomlinson
     Abraham Talbot was born on 21 November 1828 at Batley. He was the son of John Talbot and Hannah Slater. Abraham Talbot was baptized on 11 December 1828 at Zion Chapel, Dewsbury.1
Hewas a 7th son.

In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the son of John Talbot at Havercroft, Batley.2
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as a woollen rag dealer aged 22 the son of John Talbot at Havercroft, Batley.3
Abraham Talbot married Mary Barraclough in 1859.1
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at Middlethorp, Morley, as a rag merchant.4
Abraham Talbot and Henry Sheard were said to have been two of the partners in the Alexandra Mill Company.5
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at King Street, Batley, as a Manufacturer.6
In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as the head of household at Bromley Road, Soothill, as a woollen manufacturer.7
In the census of 5 April 1891 he was listed as the head of household at Ellinroyd House, Soothill, as a woollen cloth manufacturer.8
He was listed in the census of 31 March 1901 at 13 Bath Street, Batley.9

My photograph shows him with his son Charles Henry and grandson Charles Reginald.

  • Sarah Ann Talbot b. 9 Feb 1861, d. 1 Dec 1865
  • Clara Talbot+ b. 19 Sep 1862

     Winifred Nightingale was born in 1815. She was the daughter of Thomas Nightingale and Ann Wigglesworth. Winifred Nightingale was baptized on 21 April 1815 at Dewsbury.
In the census of she was listed as visiting her sisterMaria Whitworth at Kirk Hill, Soothill.1

Elizabeth Wigglesworth

#3375, b. 1770
Elizabeth Wigglesworth|b. 1770|p113.htm#i3375|William Wigglesworth||p110.htm#i3282||||||||||||||||
     Elizabeth Wigglesworth was born in 1770. She was the daughter of William Wigglesworth. Elizabeth Wigglesworth was baptized on 29 April 1770 at Dewsbury.1
She married Robert Nightingale on 9 February 1793 at Dewsbury.
On 8 June 1814 an Elizabeth Nightingale of Dewsbury buried at Gisburn as aged 82.

Children of Elizabeth Wigglesworth and Robert Nightingale

  • John Nightingale b. 10 Jun 1797
  • William Nightingale b. 1799

Citations

  1. [S11] Register Transcript, Dewsbury.

     Harriot Nightingale was born in 1810. She was the daughter of Thomas Nightingale and Ann Wigglesworth. Harriot Nightingale married Jeremiah Whitworth, son of Squire Whitworth, on 11 January 1838 at Dewsbury.1,2
