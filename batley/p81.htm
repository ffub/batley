David Fox

#2443, b. 1808, d. March 1846

Relationship=2nd cousin 5 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     David Fox was born in 1808. He was the son of John Fox and Martha Richardson. David Fox was baptized on 21 October 1808 at Old Chapel Independent, Heckmondwike.
He married Rachel Fox, daughter of Joseph Jubb and Letitia Talbot, on 27 December 1830 at Batley Witnesses Phineas Fox, Josh Chadwick.1
David Fox witnessed the marriage of John Fox and Mary Sheard on 14 February 1831 at Batley; witnesses were Joseph Jubb and David Fox.1 In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at Spa Hill, Batley, as a woollen feeder(?).2
David Fox died in March 1846. He was buried on 19 March 1846 at Batley.1

Children of David Fox and Rachel Jubb

  • Letitia Fox+ b. 1831
  • William James Richardson Fox+ b. 1833, d. 31 Jan 1920
  • Martha Jane Fox+ b. c 1837
  • Joseph Jubb Fox+ b. 17 Feb 1843, d. 1908
