Relationship=3rd cousin 3 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     Elizabeth Blakeley was the daughter of Caroline Stubley Blakeley. Elizabeth Blakeley was born on 25 October 1843.1 She was baptized on 24 December 1843 at Batley.1
In the census of 30 March 1851 she was listed as aged 7 the niece of William Blakeley.2 Elizabeth Blakeley married Christopher Richard Greenwood, son of Richard Greenwood and Sarah Tidder, in 1865.
Elizabeth Greenwood married David Sheard, son of Abraham Sheard and Maria Sheard, in 1877.3
In the census of 3 April 1881 Elizabeth Sheard was listed at Commercial Street, Batley, as wife of David Sheard.4
In the census of 5 April 1891 Elizabeth Sheard was listed at No 1 Market Place, Batley, as wife of David Sheard.5
In the census of 31 March 1901 she was listed as the head of household at 1 Market Place, Batley, as a widow, a stationer and newsagent.6
