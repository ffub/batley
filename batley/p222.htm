Elizabeth Pyrah

#6301, b. 1799, d. 6 November 1883
Elizabeth Pyrah|b. 1799\nd. 6 Nov 1883|p222.htm#i6301|Benjamin Pyrah|b. 1760\nd. 1838|p15.htm#i432|Sally Firth||p58.htm#i1679|Joshua Pyrah|d. 1796|p21.htm#i629|Sarah Beaumont|d. 1786|p21.htm#i630|John Firth||p58.htm#i1683||||

Relationship=1st cousin 6 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     Elizabeth Pyrah was born in 1799. She was the daughter of Benjamin Pyrah and Sally Firth. Elizabeth Pyrah was born on 26 March 1799.1 She was baptized on 28 April 1799 at Batley.1
She married Jeremiah Hudswell, son of Thomas Hudswell and Elizabeth Hall, on 19 January 1817 at Batley Both wrote their names clearly. The marriage was witnessed by William Parr and Thomas Lee.1
In the census of 6 June 1841 Elizabeth Hudswell was listed at Up Lane, Batley, as wife of Jeremiah Hudswell.2
In the census of 30 March 1851 Elizabeth Hudswell was listed at Up Lane, Batley, as wife of Jeremiah Hudswell.3
Elizabeth Hudswell died on 6 November 1883 at 52 Burley St, Leeds; aged 84, as relict of late Jeremiah Hudswell of Batley.4

Emma Popplewell

#6314, b. circa 1843
     Emma Popplewell was born circa 1843. I have not found a baptism for Emma. It is possible she was a daughter of Francis Popplewell which would account for her being described as Jenet Senior's niece, and a link with this family of Poplewells certainly seems likely. In the census of 30 March 1851 she was listed as aged 7 the niece of Joseph Pyrah.1 In the census of 7 April 1861 she was listed as the adopted daughter of Jenet Pyrah at Popplewell's Build, Brewery Bridge, Thornhill.2
Emma Popplewell married John Turner in 1863.3
In the census of 2 April 1871 she was listed as the niece of Jenet Pyrah.4

Children of Emma Popplewell and John Turner

Frédéric Charles Colbeck married Angélique Prudence Bureau, daughter of Eugène Achille Bureau and Sarah Rowland, on 21 July 1906 at Fulham, London.1
I have not found Frédéric Charles Colbeck and Angélique in the 1911 census, but by 1814 they were living in Horbury, where their children were born.
Frédéric Charles Colbeck died on 3 March 1925 at Pontefract at age 52.1
