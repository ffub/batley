Ellen Barber

#4602, b. circa 1834
     Ellen Barber was born circa 1834 at Woodhouse. As of 1855, Ellen Barber was also known as Mary Ellen Barber at marriage. She married Abraham Lister, son of Joseph Lister and Jenny Raper, on 4 November 1855 at Cathedral, Manchester, witnesses being Henry Brown and Mary Elliot. Abraham was described as the son of Joseph, a weaver, and Mary Ellen as the daughter of Joseph, a guard.1
In the census of 7 April 1861 Ellen Barber was listed at Crosley St, Batley, as wife of Abraham Lister.2,3
In the census of 2 April 1871 Ellen Barber was listed at Spa Hill, Batley, as wife of Abraham Lister.2,4
In the census of 3 April 1881 she was listed as the head of household at Spa Hill, Batley, a widow aged 44.2,5
In the census of 5 April 1891 she was listed as the head of household at 10 Spa Hill, Batley, as a widow aged 55.2,6
