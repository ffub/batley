     David Sheard was born on 1 May 1840. He was the son of Benjamin Sheard and Margaret Gledhill. David Sheard was baptized on 14 June 1840 at Batley.
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the son of Benjamin Sheard at Havercroft, Batley.1
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the son of Benjamin Sheard at Havercroft, Batley.2
David Sheard married Caroline Jackson on 20 September 1860 at Batley.3
In the census of 7 April 1861 David Sheard, listed as a lodger, appeared as a rag machine overlooker, now married to Caroline, in the household of his mother Margaret Sheard at Old Mill Lane, Batley.4

In 1862 David and Caroline Sheard emigrated to America with their daughter, sailing from Liverpool to New York on the "Constitution of Liverpool" and arriving on the 27th August.5
In the census of 1870 he was listed as the head of household at Newburgh, Orange, New York, as a weaver.6
In the census of 8 June 1880 he was listed as the head of household at Philadelphia, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, as a carpet weaver.7
David Sheard died on 14 January 1899 at Philadelphia at age 58.

  • Eliza Ann Roberts Sheard b. 1861
