Abraham Stubley

#1505, b. 1737, d. 1802
Abraham Stubley|b. 1737\nd. 1802|p51.htm#i1505|Thomas Stubley|b. 1703|p20.htm#i627|Sarah Williamson|d. 1787|p16.htm#i487|John Stubley|d. Jan 1713/14|p57.htm#i1663|Susanna (?)|d. Mar 1709/10|p62.htm#i1875|||||||

Relationship=6th great-uncle of Vivien Eley.
     Abraham Stubley was born in 1737 at Otley. He was the son of Thomas Stubley and Sarah Williamson.
I believe Abraham Stubley was the one baptized on 8 April 1737 at Otley son of Thomas of Burleywoodhead, clothier. I would point out that this is the ONLY Stubley entry in these records. There is nothing to suggest that this Thomas, who was a clothier, was born, married, had children or died here. Abraham's age would fit with that of the one who married Noah Eastwood in 1757.1
He married Noah Eastwood, daughter of William Eastwood, on 19 December 1757 at Batley.2
Abraham Stubley together with his brother could have witnessed the marriage of Hannah Stubley and Jeremiah Chadwick on 4 September 1780 at Batley as uncles.3 Abraham Stubley died in 1802 at Batley.
