George Eayrs

#4848, b. 1864, d. 1926
     George Eayrs was born in 1864. He was the officiating Minister. He was the author of several books on Methodism, including "A History of Zion Church, Batley, and the Churches of the United Methodist Batley Circuit" published in 1909 This makes mention of the eighteenth century history of Methoodism in the district, of visits by Wesley himself and the work of his followers including John Nelson of Birstall, Mostly, though, it is an account of the origins of these Churches themselves, with accounts of some of those local people who were their stalwarts.1 He died in 1926.

Citations

  1. [S239] F.R. Hist.S. Rev. George Eayrs A History of Zion Church and Batley United Methodist Churches.
