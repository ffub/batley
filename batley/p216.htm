In the census of 5 April 1930 Martha Sheard was listed at 64 Jackson Street, Little Falls, Herkimer, New York, as wife of Isaac Sheard.5
Martha Duesler, having outlived her husband by thirty years, died on 26 August 1965 at Little Falls at age 96.1

     Lois Hall was born on 14 July 1868.1 She was the daughter of Walter Hall and Matilda Colbeck. In the census of 2 April 1871 she was listed as the daughter of Walter Hall at Commercial Street, Batley.2
In the census of 3 April 1881 she was listed as the daughter of Walter Hall at Bradford Road, Batley.3
In the census of 5 April 1891 she was listed as visiting her grandmother Elizabeth Colbeck at Bath Street, Batley.
Lois Hall married William Haigh Thomson on 14 May 1894 at St. George's Church, Leeds.1

     Elizabeth Colbeck Hall was born on 14 February 1870.1 She was the daughter of Walter Hall and Matilda Colbeck. Elizabeth Colbeck Hall was baptized on 17 March 1870 at Batley.1

In the census of 2 April 1871 she was listed as the daughter of Walter Hall at Commercial Street, Batley.2
In the census of 3 April 1881 she was listed as the daughter of Walter Hall at Bradford Road, Batley.3
In the census of 5 April 1891 she was listed as the daughter of Walter Hall at 48 Springfield Rd, Leeds.4
Elizabeth Colbeck Hall married Frank Smithson in 1892 at Leeds.5
