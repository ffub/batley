William Wood

#2387
     William Wood married Harriot Blakeley, daughter of Joseph Blakeley and Mary Sheard, on 22 November 1813 at Batley witnesses Thomas Parr and Jeremiah Chadwick.1
William Wood was described as a butcher at the baptisms of his children, but seems to have moved from Batley to Dewsbury between the births of Joseph in 1819 and Mary in 1821.

Children of William Wood and Harriot Blakeley

  • Elizabeth Wood b. 1816
  • Joseph Blakeley Wood b. 1819
  • Mary Wood b. 1821
  • Benjamin Blakeley Wood b. 1822

Hannah Hepworth

     Hannah Hepworth was born circa 1813 at Ireland.1 She was the daughter of John Hepworth and Margaret (?). Hannah Hepworth married William Sheard, son of George Sheard and Ann Greenwood, on 4 February 1836 at Batley both made marks Witnesses Joseph Fell and Samuel Scholes.2

Hannah Sheard was listed on the census of 6 June 1841 at Havercroft, Batley, as wife of William Sheard.3
In the census of 30 March 1851 Hannah Sheard was listed at Havercroft, Batley, as wife of William Sheard.4
In the census of 7 April 1861 Hannah Sheard was listed at "quary", Batley, as wife of William Sheard.5
Hannah Sheard was listed aged 56 on the census of 2 April 1871 at Ward's Hill, Batley, as wife of William Sheard.1
In the census of 3 April 1881 she was listed as the head of household at off Wellington St, Batley, as a widow aged 66.6

Ann Sheard married Joseph Buxton in 1857 deduced that this is correct marriage from presence of Ann Buxton with her parents in 1871.3
In the census of 7 April 1861 Ann Buxton was listed at Fox Row, Batley, as wife of Joseph Buxton.4
In the census of 2 April 1871 she was listed as a widow, a woollen weaver aged 34 the daughter of William Sheard at Ward's Hill, Batley.5
In the census of 3 April 1881 she was listed as a widow, a woollen weaver aged 44 the daughter of Hannah Sheard at off Wellington St, Batley.6
Ann Sheard died in 1889.7,8

William Akeroyd Jubb

#2416, b. 1833, d. 12 September 1876
William Akeroyd Jubb|b. 1833\nd. 12 Sep 1876|p79.htm#i2416|John Jubb|b. 1807\nd. 20 Mar 1888|p75.htm#i2238|Mary Akeroyd|b. 21 Mar 1812|p54.htm#i1589|Joseph Jubb|b. 2 Jul 1784\nd. 24 Jan 1867|p75.htm#i2237|Letitia Talbot|b. 2 Apr 1787\nd. 22 Oct 1831|p49.htm#i1430|John Akeroyd|d. 30 Aug 1839|p18.htm#i522|Jane G. Relationship=2nd cousin 4 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     William Akeroyd Jubb was born in 1833. He was the son of John Jubb and Mary Akeroyd. William Akeroyd Jubb was baptized on 7 April 1833 at Batley.1
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the son of John Jubb at Providence Place, Batley.2
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as an assistant the son of John Jubb at Providence St, Batley.3
William Akeroyd Jubb married Mary Lightowler in 1856.4
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at Providence Street, Batley, as a Shoddy merchant employing 19 men and 16 women.5
In 1868 William Akeroyd Jubb was was made a Captain in the 43rd West Riding Rifle Volunteers.6
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at Providence Street, Batley, as a Shoddy merchant and manufacturer.7
He died on 12 September 1876.
