     Angélique Prudence Bureau was born on 17 May 1880 at Le Havre, France.1
She was the daughter of Eugène Achille Bureau and Sarah Rowland. Angélique Prudence Bureau married Frédéric Charles Colbeck, son of Edwin Colbeck and Jane Whitworth, on 21 July 1906 at Fulham, London.1

Rachel Chadwick

#6337, b. circa 1819
Rachel Chadwick|b. c 1819|p223.htm#i6337|Thomas Chadwick||p304.htm#i8433||||||||||||||||
     Rachel Chadwick was born circa 1819 at Dewsbury. She was the daughter of Thomas Chadwick. Rachel Chadwick was baptized on 25 June 1819 at Dewsbury.1
She married Benjamin Hall, son of Joseph Hall and Elizabeth Taylor, on 13 February 1840 at Dewsbury I think it was this Benjamin who married Rachel, as they were much of an age, and his early death would fit with her remarriage.2,1
In the census of 6 June 1841 Rachel Hall was listed at Eightlands, Dewsbury, as wife of Benjamin Hall.3
Rachel Hall married William Exley, son of Samuel Exley and Elizabeth Nelson, on 2 October 1845 at Batley Witnesses were William Hall and John Hepworth.4
In the census of 30 March 1851 Rachel Exley was listed at Healey, Batley, as wife of William Exley.5
In the census of 7 April 1861 she was listed as the head of household at 235 Henry Street, Dewsbury, as a beer house keeper, a widow.6
Rachel Exley was buried on 7 June 1888.7
