Mary Ann (?)

#1742, b. circa 1815
     Mary Ann (?) was born circa 1815. She married George Newsome, son of Samuel Newsome and Sarah Stubley.
In the census of 6 June 1841 Mary Ann Newsome was listed at Havercroft, Batley, as wife of George Newsome.1
Mary Ann (?) may be the Mary Ann Newsome to be found as a sick nurse in the household of Edward Day at Hirst House, Batley in 1851.
