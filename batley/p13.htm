Relationship=2nd cousin 5 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     Sarah Blakeley was born in 1814. She was the daughter of Abraham Blakeley and Martha Colbeck. Sarah Blakeley was baptized on 11 December 1814 at Batley.
In the census of 7 April 1861 she was listed as the head of household at Commercial Street, Batley, as a grocer aged 46.1
Sarah Blakeley's shop was probably the one taken over by Abraham Lister.
