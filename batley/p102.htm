     Charles Arthur Sheard was born on 19 March 1865 at Batley. He was the son of Richard Arthur Sheard and Ellen Whitworth. In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as aged 6 the son of Richard Arthur Sheard at Liversedge, Roberttown.1
Charles Arthur Sheard emigrated in 1887 to New York; described as a clerk and sailing on the Celtic from Liverpool and arriving in New York on the first of March 1887.2 He married Nellie Lorraine Ives, daughter of James D Ives and Sarah Ives, on 10 October 1889 at Little Falls, Herkimer, New York.3
In the census of June 1900 he was listed as the head of household.
In the census of April 1910 he was listed as the head of household at Little Falls, Herkimer, New York, as a salesman, raw material.4
Charles Arthur Sheard was naturalized as an US citizen on 17 June 1917. He married Harriet Bunnell in October 1917.
In the census of 13 January 1920 he was listed as the head of household at Little Falls, Herkimer, New York, as a salesman, woollen goods.5
Charles Arthur Sheard died on 22 November 1940 at Little Falls, NY, at age 75.6
