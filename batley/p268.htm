Mary Roberts

#7493, b. 2 October 1832, d. 16 June 1895
     Mary Roberts was born on 2 October 1832.1
Mary Roberts married William Rawden, son of Benjamin Rawden and Charlotte Preston, in 1851.2
In 1852 Mary Rawden and William Rawden with their infant son travelled to America sailing on the Huguenot from Liverpool to New York and arriving on the 10th of June.3
In the census of 2 April 1871 Mary Rawden was listed at Cemetery Road, Batley, as wife of William Rawden.4
In the census of 3 April 1881 Mary Rawden was listed at Cross Bank Street, Batley, as wife of William Rawden.5
In the census of 5 April 1891 she was listed as the head of household at 1 Cemetery Road, Batley, as a widow living on her own means.6
Mary Roberts died on 16 June 1895 at age 62.1
