#3627
     Jane Hirst married Abraham Preston, son of John Preston and Martha Marshall, on 10 March 1844 at Dewsbury Both made their marks. Witnesses were Joseph Ward and Isaac Armitage.1

In the census of 7 April 1861 Jane Preston was listed at Beckside, Batley, as wife of Abraham Preston.3
In the census of 2 April 1871 she was listed as in the household of Abraham Preston at Blue Hill, Batley.
