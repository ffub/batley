Joseph Buxton

#4551, b. 13 November 1833, d. 1867
     Joseph Buxton was born on 13 November 1833 at Batley.1 He was baptized on 26 January 1834 at Salem Chapel, Hunslet Lne Independent, Leeds.1
He married Ann Sheard, daughter of William Sheard and Hannah Hepworth, in 1857 deduced that this is correct marriage from presence of Ann Buxton with her parents in 1871.2
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at Fox Row, Batley, as a cloth fuller aged 27. A nurse, Nancy Dawson, 26, from Mirfield is also present.3
Joseph Buxton died in 1867.1,4
