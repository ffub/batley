Jane Fox

     Jane Fox was born in 1816. She was the daughter of James Fox and Jane Castle. Jane Fox was baptized on 7 April 1817 at Batley.1
She married Richard Stubley, son of Joseph Stubley and Mary Denton, on 7 March 1836 at Batley witnesses Thomas Stubley, John Chadwick.1
In the census of 6 June 1841 she was listed as the daughter of James Fox at Havercroft, Batley.2
In the census of 30 March 1851 Jane Stubley was listed at Havercroft, Batley, as wife of Richard Stubley.3
In the census of 7 April 1861 Jane Stubley was listed at Wellington Street, Batley, as wife of Richard Stubley.4
In the census of 2 April 1871 Jane Stubley was listed at 12 Wellington Street, Batley, as wife of Richard Stubley.5
In the census of 3 April 1881 she was listed as the head of household at off Wellington St, Batley, Living with Jane is grandson George Stubley, a woollen cloth dressere.6
