Jeremiah Chadwick

#5285, b. circa 1828, d. 12 March 1879
Jeremiah Chadwick|b. c 1828\nd. 12 Mar 1879|p183.htm#i5285|John Chadwick|b. 3 Jan 1807\nd. 5 Nov 1850|p54.htm#i1569|Susanna Wainwright|b. c 1807\nd. 23 Jan 1834|p149.htm#i4401|Jeremiah Chadwick|b. 20 Feb 1781\nd. 6 Apr 1816|p134.htm#i4006|Mary Clapham|b. 1786\nd. 28 Jul 1837|p53.htm#i1566|||||||
     Jeremiah Chadwick was born circa 1828. He was the son of John Chadwick and Susanna Wainwright. Jeremiah Chadwick was baptized on 26 October 1828 as son of John and Susan.1
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as aged 12, I believe staying with his great-uncle in the household of Joseph Chadwick at Up Lane, Batley.2
Jeremiah Chadwick died on 12 March 1879; at the Victoria Inn, Batley, aged 50.

#5290, b. circa 1823

Elizabeth Cole

#5306, b. 2 January 1824, d. 1899
     Elizabeth Cole was born on 2 January 1824. On 31 December 1843 Elizabeth Cole married George Markham Tweddell at the Parish Church, Stokesley.
In a letter to Isaac Binns George Markham said of his wife Elizabeth Cole "Those who labour under the gross delusion that literary tastes and talents are incompatible with household duties and domestic affections, need only know Mrs G.M. Tweddell to be convinced of their error."; with a biography of his wife, Elizabeth, whose work Isaac must have been interested in publishing.
Elizabeth Tweddell died in 1899.
