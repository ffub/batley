Mary Senior

#5055, b. circa 1834
Mary Senior|b. c 1834|p174.htm#i5055|Frank Senior||p305.htm#i8457||||||||||||||||
     Mary Senior was born circa 1834. She was the daughter of Frank Senior. Mary Senior was baptized on 11 April 1834 at Birstall.1
She married Sheard Newsome, son of George Newsome and Elizabeth Reyner, on 26 December 1851 at Tong Sheard gives his residence as Birstall and Mary as Heckmondwike. Witnesses were Frank Senior and David Fox.2
In the census of 2 April 1871 Mary Newsome was listed at 46 Garbut St, Middlesbrough, as wife of Sheard Newsome.
In the census of 1880 Mary Newsome was listed at Manchester, Hillsborough, New Hampshire, as wife of Sheard Newsome.3

Children of Mary Senior and Sheard Newsome

  • Pamela Newsome b. 1851
  • John Newsome b. 14 Jul 1852
  • Joseph Newsome b. c 1855
  • Walter Newsome b. c 1859

     Joseph Colbeck was born circa 1853 at Batley. He was the son of John Colbeck and Jane (?). Joseph Colbeck married Elinor Hargreaves, daughter of Barnabas Hargreaves and Ann Blackburn, in 1870.1

Joseph Colbeck married Mary Ann Sykes in 1895.5
In the census of 31 March 1901 he was listed as the head of household at 83 Commercial Street (Crown Hotel", Batley, as an innkeeper.6
