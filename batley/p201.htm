Hannah Morrell

#5756, b. circa 1834
     Hannah Morrell was born circa 1834. She married Thomas Brearley, son of Robert Brearley and Elizabeth Burnill, on 27 May 1856 at Spofforth.1
In the census of 7 April 1861 Hannah Brearley was listed at King Street, Batley, as wife of Thomas Brearley.2
In the census of 2 April 1871 Hannah Brearley was listed at Oakwood House, Upper Batley, Batley, as wife of Thomas Brearley.3
In the census of 3 April 1881 Hannah Brearley was listed at Oakwood House, Upper Batley Lane, Batley, as wife of Thomas Brearley.4,5

Children of Hannah Morrell and Thomas Brearley

  • Arthur Brearley b. c 1858
  • Robert Brearley+ b. 1861
  • Henry Brearley b. c 1864
  • Thomas Percival Brearley b. 1868

Frank Colbeck

#5771, b. 31 May 1861, d. 9 January 1941
Frank Colbeck|b. 31 May 1861\nd. 9 Jan 1941|p201.htm#i5771|William Henry Colbeck|b. 1829\nd. 25 Jun 1901|p145.htm#i4294|Martha Cordingley|d. 4 Jun 1897|p154.htm#i4558|William Colbeck|b. 6 Feb 1783\nd. 19 Oct 1849|p77.htm#i2290|Elizabeth Richardson|b. c 1785\nd. 3 Dec 1856|p106.htm#i3160|||||||
     Frank Colbeck was born on 31 May 1861 at Batley.1 He was the son of William Henry Colbeck and Martha Cordingley. Frank Colbeck was baptized on 30 June 1861 at Batley.1
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the son of William Henry Colbeck at West House, Bradford Road, Gomersal.2
He was listed in the census of 3 April 1881 as a Gentleman Cadet at The Royal Military College,, Sandhurst, Berkshire. He is said to have passed out with honours.3,1
On 4 October 1882 Frank Colbeck was commissioned into the 1st West India Regiment.1
On 26 March 1884 Frank Colbeck resigned his commission.1
He married Flora McDonald on 22 December 1887 at St Mark's Church, Remuera, New Zealand.1
Three years after his first wife's early death Frank Colbeck married Emma McGlashan on 19 August 1891 at St Barnabas'Church, Mt Eden, Auckland.1
Frank Colbeck died on 9 January 1941 at Auckland, New Zealand, at age 79.
