Grace Newsome

#5907, b. circa 1830
Grace Newsome|b. c 1830|p206.htm#i5907|William Newsome||p308.htm#i8531||||||||||||||||
     Grace Newsome was born circa 1830. She was the daughter of William Newsome. Grace Newsome married Abraham Stubley, son of Charles Stubley and Hannah Mason, on 11 November 1850 at the Parish Church, Dewsbury, Abraham was described as of Batley Carr and Grace as of Dewsbury Moor.1

Children of Grace Newsome and Abraham Stubley

  • William Stubley b. c 1852
  • Albert Stubley b. c 1863
  • Newsome Stubley b. c 1865
  • Charles Stubley+ b. c 1870
