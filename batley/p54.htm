Ada Sheard

#1591, b. 23 August 1860, d. 9 May 1952

     Ada Sheard was born on 23 August 1860 at Batley.1 She was the daughter of Joseph Sheard and Sarah Ann Colbeck. Ada Sheard was baptized on 20 September 1860 at Batley.1
In the census of 7 April 1861 she was listed as aged 7 months the daughter of Joseph Sheard at Sunnybank House, Upper Batley, Batley.2
In the census of 2 April 1871 she was listed as aged 10 the daughter of Joseph Sheard at Sunnybank House, Batley.3
In the census of 3 April 1881 she was listed as aged 20 the daughter of Sarah Ann Colbeck at Spring Grove House, Field Hill, Batley.4
In 1881 Ada Sheard was a bridesmaid for her cousin Fanny Sheard.5
Ada Sheard married George Bennet Jubb, son of George Jubb and Rachel Field, on 16 August 1882 at St. Thomas's, Batley.
Ada Jubb accompanied her husband to Austria where he was superintending the firm of Jubb and Unna in which his brother was a partner.
Ada and George Bennet Jubb were living in Fischamend when a dreadful incident occurred in which George was injured.
In the census of 5 April 1891 Ada Sheard was listed at Ebury Street, Batley, as wife of George Bennet Jubb.6
In the census of 31 March 1901 Ada Jubb was listed at 29 Carlinghow Lane, Batley, as wife of George Bennet Jubb.7
In the census of 2 April 1911 she was listed as the head of household at 29 Carlinghow Lane, Batley, as a widow aged 50.8
Ada Sheard died on 9 May 1952 at age 91.9

Children of Ada Sheard and George Bennet Jubb

  • George Bertrand Jubb b. c 1885
  • Joseph Jubb b. c 1886
  • Aline M Jubb b. c 1888
  • Fred A Jubb b. c 1889
  • Charles E Jubb b. c 1892
  • Kathleen Alice Jubb b. c 1894
  • William Jubb b. c 1896
