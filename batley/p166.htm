     Marmaduke Fox was born in 1800. He was the son of Marmaduke Fox and Sarah Fox.
Marmaduke Fox was baptized on 19 November 1800 at Dewsbury.1
He married Elizabeth Newsome on 7 October 1821 at Batley witnesses Thos Newsome and Jer. Chadwick.2

Child of Marmaduke Fox and Elizabeth Newsome

  • Marmaduke Fox b. 18 Sep 1822
