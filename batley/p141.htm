     John Burnley was born on 2 June 1805 at Batley.1 He was the son of John Burnley and Elizabeth Blakeley. John Burnley was baptized on 21 July 1805 at Batley.1
He married Harriet Fox, daughter of Phineas Fox and Elizabeth (?), on 22 March 1827 at Dewsbury she was of Soothill at marriage.
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at Havercroft, Batley, as a woollen manufacturer aged 35. Something of a puzzle here is that this census shows John with a Jane, also shown as 35. His children were baptised as those of John and Harriet, so I think this may be a mistake. Another possiblity is that Harriet was away, (though I cannot find her elsewhere) and some other relative was helping with the children.2
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Havercroft, Batley, as a woollen manufacturer employing 82 men, 16 women, 24 boys & 17 girls.3
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed at Burnley's Mill Fold, Batley, as a woollen flock dealer aged 55.4
