Shepley Watson

#5478, b. 1759
Shepley Watson|b. 1759|p190.htm#i5478|John Watson||p174.htm#i5077|Mary Shepley|b. 1727|p148.htm#i4396|||||||Jeremiah Shepley|b. 1699\nd. 13 Feb 1745/46|p93.htm#i2821|Jane Raynor|b. c 1684\nd. 19 Dec 1748|p94.htm#i2837|

Relationship=2nd cousin 6 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     Shepley Watson was born in 1759. He was the son of John Watson and Mary Shepley. Shepley Watson was baptized on 21 May 1759 at Felkirk.1
Shepley Watson was lawyer and estate agent to the Winns of Nostell Priory. Esther, the sister of the 6th baronet, Sir Rowland Winn, had caused a family scandal by eloping with the local baker. She died in 1803, but her children were taken in hand by her unmarried brother and their education is said to have been supervised by Shepley at his home at Cold Hiendly.2
