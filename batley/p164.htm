Thomas Hirst

#4799, b. 1790
Thomas Hirst|b. 1790|p164.htm#i4799|Job Hirst||p118.htm#i3525|Christiana Carr|b. 1763|p120.htm#i3579|Jonas Hirst||p120.htm#i3580||||Samuel Carr||p120.htm#i3577||||
     Thomas Hirst was born in 1790 at Dewsbury. He was the son of Job Hirst and Christiana Carr. Thomas Hirst was baptized on 5 April 1790 at Dewsbury.1
He married Mary (?).
Thomas, James Hall's brother-in-law, is likely to have been the Thomas Hirst Willans associated with Purlwell Mill
; said to have been the builder of Purlwell Mill, though Willans associated him with his brothers and also Robert Brearley senior and.2 In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at Purlwell, Batley, as independent. there is a female servant, Mary Farnhill aged 15.3
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Purlwell, Batley, as a proprietor of houses. there is a female servant, Christiana Harrison aged 17.4
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as a proprietor of houses living with his widowed sister Martha Hall at Purlwell, Batley.5

#4824, b. circa 1819
     Benjamin Buckley was born circa 1819 at Leeds. He married Mary Blakeley, daughter of Abraham Blakeley and Martha Colbeck, on 11 November 1838 at Wakefield I believe this to be Mary Blakeley born 1819 as in the 1861 census a George Buckley is living with her sister Sarah as nephew.1
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Havercroft North side, Batley, a hand loom weaver aged 32.2

Children of Benjamin Buckley and Mary Blakeley

  • Hannah Buckley b. c 1840
  • George Buckley b. c 1846
