Descendants of Joseph Hall (#1)
Descendants of Joseph Hall (#2)
     Alexander Hall was born on 10 November 1847 at Batley.1 He was the son of Thomas Hall and Martha Hall. Alexander Hall was baptized on 26 December 1847 at Batley.1
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as a scholar the son of Thomas Hall at Clerk Green, Batley.2
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as a scholar the son of Thomas Hall at Clerk Green, Batley.3
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as assistant (presumably to his father) the son of Thomas Hall at Purlwell Lane, Batley.4
Alexander Hall married Charlotte Preston, daughter of Joseph Preston, on 4 February 1875 at Batley.5
By 1878 Alexander's father had died, but he was listed as in business with his brothers as "Thomas Hall's Sons" at Station Road and Providence Mills.6
In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as the head of household at Providence Street, Batley, as a woollen manufacturer. Living with Alexander are housekeeper Ann Scott and her four daughters.7
