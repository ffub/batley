John Fearnsides

#2441, b. 28 February 1804

Relationship=1st cousin 5 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     John Fearnsides was born on 28 February 1804.1 He was the son of Robert Fearnsides and Mary Spedding. John Fearnsides was baptized on 15 March 1804 at Batley.1
He married Hannah Hemingway, daughter of Jacob Hemingway and Hannah Dean, on 24 March 1828 at Batley Described as John Fearsides of Dewbury and Hannah Hemingway of Batley. witnesses were Thomas Dean and James Knowles.1
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at Westgate, Dewsbury, as a butcher. There was a female servant Ann Parker aged 20.2
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Daisy Hill, Dewsbury, as a butcher, a widower,There is a servant, Mary Ann Haigh aged 16.3

Children of John Fearnsides and Hannah Hemingway

  • Charles Fearnsides b. c 1829
  • Mary Hemingway Fearnsides b. c 1831
  • Thomas Fearnsides b. c 1833

Martha Richardson

#2442, b. 1788
     Martha Richardson was born in 1788. She married John Fox, son of Phineas Fox and Jane Blakeley, on 26 December 1806 at Batley.1
In the census of 6 June 1841 she was listed as the head of household at Batley as in the woollen trade.2
In the census of 30 March 1851 she was listed as the head of household at North side Commercial Street, Havercroft, Batley, as a widow aged 62, one of 4 proprietors of a woollen mill and freehold houses.3
