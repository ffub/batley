Philip Crawshaw

#4876, b. circa 1771, d. 26 October 1843
     Philip Crawshaw was born circa 1771; from age at death. He married Susanna Senior, daughter of Squire Senior, on 6 September 1795 at Dewsbury Philip is described as a carrier.1
In 1834 Philip Crawshaw was listed as the landlord of the "Crown and Cushion" in the Market Place at Dewsbury.2
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at Long Causeway, Dewsbury, as independent.3
He died on 26 October 1843; described as for many years landlord of the "Crown and Cushion" Dewsbury.4

Children of Philip Crawshaw and Susanna Senior

  • Simon Crawshaw b. 1805
  • Philip Crawshaw b. c 1807, d. b Apr 1812
  • Joseph Crawshaw b. 1808
  • Elizabeth Crawshaw+ b. 16 Jun 1809
  • Philip Crawshaw b. 1812
  • Martha Crawshaw b. 1817
