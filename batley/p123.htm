Norman William Eley

#3723, b. 29 April 1874, d. 10 January 1917

Relationship=Grandfather of Vivien Eley.
Charts
Ancestry of Vivien Eley Tomlinson
Norman Eley with my father, his eldest son. collection V. E. Tomlinson
     Norman William Eley was born on 29 April 1874 at Church Gresley, Derbyshire. He was baptized on 23 June 1874 at the Wesleyan Methodist Chapel, Swadlincote.
He was educated at Burton upon Trent Grammar School, then studied Medicine at Mason's College, Birmingham. Since Birmingham did not yet have University status his degrees were from Durham. He completed his studies in 1896, and then spent some time at Hetton-le-Hole in County Durham.
He was in Batley by 1898 and present at the wedding of Dr Herbert Keighley to Minnie Wilson.
He was listed in the census of 31 March 1901 at 100 Wellington Street, Batley, lodging with Dr. John Woods, by whom he had recently been engaged as a an assistant.1 He was soon taken into partnership with Dr. Woods and Dr. Herbert Keighley.

Norman William Eley married Ann Eliza Blackburn, daughter of John William Blackburn and Eliza Rebecca Sheard, on 20 December 1906 at Zion Chapel, Batley, The wedding took place shortly before Christmas, and was a small one, but we are told that "Both Dr. Eley and Miss Blackburn were well known, and though the ceremony was of a semi-private nature, a considerable number of people were present to see the nuptial knot tied.2

Few photographs survive from early married days, but some capture a glimpse of happy times.

Following the Insurance Act of in 1911 the Medical partnership was dissolved by mutual agreement and he set up in practice on his own account. Norman William Eley was appointed to the Hon. Medical Staff of Batley and District Hospital in March 1915.
The Medical Directory of that year lists him in practice at The Red House.
He died on 10 January 1917 at Batley at age 42. He was buried on 13 January 1917 at the cemetery, Batley.

Children of Norman William Eley and Ann Eliza Blackburn

  • Brian Eley+ b. 18 Apr 1908, d. 28 Apr 1997
  • James Alan Eley b. Mar 1916, d. 5 Jan 1995
