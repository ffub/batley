Kate Newell

#7173, b. circa 1869, d. 26 August 1966
Kate Newell|b. c 1869\nd. 26 Aug 1966|p255.htm#i7173|Charles A Newell|b. c 1835|p255.htm#i7174|Maria (?)|b. c 1840|p255.htm#i7175|||||||||||||
     Kate Newell was born circa 1869 at New York. She was the daughter of Charles A Newell and Maria (?). Kate Newell married Frank Sheard, son of Samuel Sheard and Agnes Sheard, in 1899.
In the census of April 1910 Kate Sheard was listed at Greece, Monroe, New York, as wife of Frank Sheard.1
In 1926 Kate and Frank made a visit to England, sailing from Montreal on the Aurania and arriving in Liverpool on June 13th, giving their destination as Pontefract where Frank's brother Joe had been living.
They returned to New York in August on the Adriatic, arriving there on the 29th.2
In the census of 2 April 1930 Kate Sheard was listed at 265 Burley Road, Rochester, Monroe, New York, as wife of Frank Sheard.3
Kate Newell died on 26 August 1966.
