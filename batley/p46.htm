George Sheard

#1365, b. 1673, d. 1751
George Sheard|b. 1673\nd. 1751|p46.htm#i1365|John Sheard|d. 1690|p46.htm#i1362|Elizabeth Armitage|d. 1721|p46.htm#i1363|||||||||||||

Relationship=8th great-uncle of Vivien Eley.
     George Sheard was born in 1673 at Briestwell. He was the son of John Sheard and Elizabeth Armitage. George Sheard was baptized on 9 February 1672/73 at Thornhill.1
He married Sarah Fisher on 4 February 1712/13 at Thornhill.
George Sheard died in 1751. He was buried on 14 September 1751 at Thornhill.
He left a will dated 29/1 /1750 There is a short inventory which mentions one cow, a crop of corn, hay in the barn, manure in the fold, and furniture including 6 chairs, a table two beds, several chairs, a pewter case, etc.

The identification of his brothers Benjamin Sheard and Michael Sheard with the ones who are later to be found in Birstall Parish is based on the fact that George Sheard's will mentions George son of his brother Michael and Benjamin son of his brother Benjamin, and this Michael and Benjamin have sons of appropriate names,whilst dates fit well. There is no further trace of them in Briestwell.
It should not, however, be regarded as proven.2

Child of George Sheard and Sarah Fisher

  • George Sheard b. 1716, d. 1718
