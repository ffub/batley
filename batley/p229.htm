Rose Hannah Holden

#6479, b. circa 1858
Rose Hannah Holden|b. c 1858|p229.htm#i6479|Joseph Holden||p303.htm#i8408||||||||||||||||
     Rose Hannah Holden was born circa 1858 at Suffolk. She was the daughter of Joseph Holden. Rose Hannah Holden married Benjamin Francis Thompson Sheard, son of John Sheard and Elizabeth Thompson, on 28 September 1878 at Batley.1
In the census of 3 April 1881 she was listed as a woollen weaver the daughter-in-law of John Sheard.2 In the census of 5 April 1891 Rose Hannah Sheard was listed at 35 Field Lane, Batley, as a woollen weaver, wife of Benjamin Francis Thompson Sheard.3
In the census of 31 March 1901 Rose Hannah Sheard was listed at 37 Field Lane, Batley, as wife of Benjamin Francis Thompson Sheard.4
