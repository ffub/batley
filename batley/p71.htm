     Peter Lister Sheard was born in 1831. He was the son of Benjamin Sheard and Margaret Gledhill. Peter Lister Sheard was baptized on 12 June 1831 at Batley.
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the son of Benjamin Sheard at Havercroft, Batley.1
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the son of Benjamin Sheard at Havercroft, Batley.2
Peter Lister Sheard , described as a cloth fuller, married Elizabeth Speight on 23 December 1852 at Dewsbury he signed, she made her mark. Witnesses were Stephen Smithson Gill and Paul Ward who both signed.3,4
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at Newsome's Yard, Batley, rag dealer, wife's name given as Ann, aged 20 and born in Doncaster. Ann remains a mystery, as we can find no record of such a marriage.5
Peter Lister Sheard married Ellen O'Brien.
In the census of 1 June 1880 he was listed as the head of household at Hartford, Hartford, Connecticut, as a carpet weaver aged 52.6
In 1884 Peter Lister Sheard took out American Citizenship at Court of Common pleas, Hartford, Connecticut.7
