Relationship=1st cousin 3 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     Jonathan Allot Brearley was born on 22 November 1850 at Batley.1 He was the son of Thomas Brearley and Ann Robinson. Jonathan Allot Brearley was baptized on 16 February 1851 at Batley.1
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as aged 10 the son of Thomas Brearley at Wheatcroft, Batley.2
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as a woollen spinner the son of Thomas Brearley at Purlwell Lane, Batley.3
Jonathan Allot Brearley married Charlotte Elizabeth Benson in 1877.4
Jonathan Allot Brearley died in 1909 at 5 Rink Terrace, Mount St, Batley.
He was buried on 11 April 1909 at Batley Cemetery (an unknown value).5
Jonathan Allot Brearley's grave is No C468 on the unconsecrated side.5

In the census of 2 April 1871 Martha Sheard was listed at High St, Batley, as wife of Joseph Sheard.4
