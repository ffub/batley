Simon Day

     Simon Day was born in 1825. He was the son of Sarah Day. Simon Day was baptized on 26 December 1825 at Dewsbury.
He was listed in the census of 6 June 1841 at Batley Carr, Batley, as an apprentice, but is believed to be Sarah's son; as a woollen manufacturer. Also present is Simon Day, 15, an apprentice.1 In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as a manufacturer, cloth,, the grandson of Rebecca Day at Hanging Heaton.2
Simon Day married Matilda Darley in 1853.3
