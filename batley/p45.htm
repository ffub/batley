William Brooke

#1334, b. 1806, d. 13 July 1880
William Brooke|b. 1806\nd. 13 Jul 1880|p45.htm#i1334|John Brooke||p255.htm#i7182|Elizabeth (?)||p290.htm#i8082|||||||||||||
     William Brooke was born in 1806 at Soothill. He was the son of John Brooke and Elizabeth (?). William Brooke was baptized on 26 February 1806 at Dewsbury.1

William Brooke married Sarah Sheard, daughter of Michael Sheard and Sarah Lister, on 29 September 1828 at Batley witnesses David Fox and Joseph Chadwick.2
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at Havercroft, Batley, as a cordwainer.3
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Havercroft, Batley, widower aged 44, a Rag dealer, 6 labourers.4
In 1855 William Brooke received a legacy of £100 in will of brother in law George Sheard.
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at Hick Lane, Batley, a widower, a rag dealer employing 20 hands.5
He married Jane Talbot Fell, daughter of John Fell and Esther Talbot, on 13 September 1861 at Salem Chapel, Dewsbury, witnesses were Benjamin Senior and Edward Taylor.6
He was listed as the head of household in the 2 April 1871 census at Caledonia House, Caledonia Road, Batley, as a Rag Merchant and Town Councillor.7
On 9 November 1873 William Brooke was elected Mayor of Batley, and was to serve a second term the following year.
He died on 13 July 1880.8

Jeremiah Crowther

#1342, b. circa 1819, d. 6 December 1889
     Jeremiah Crowther was born circa 1819 at England. He married Hannah Mortimer, daughter of William Mortimer and Betty Hopkinson, on 18 November 1838 at Calverley.
Jeremiah Crowther and Hannah Crowther emigrated to America in 1840 on the "Citizen" from Liverpool, arriving in New York on May 27th. With them was George Crowther, aged 17.1
In the census of 27 August 1850 he was listed as the head of household at Philadelphia as a spinner aged 31, listed as Crother.2
In the census of 24 July 1860 he was listed as the head of household at Philadelphia as a spooler aged 40, listed as Crother.3
In the census of 13 June 1870 he was listed as the head of household at Philadelphia aged 51, employed in a woollen mill.4
In the census of 1 June 1880 he was listed as the head of household at Philadelphia, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, as a spinner.5
Jeremiah Crowther died on 6 December 1889 at Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. He was buried on 9 December 1889 at Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. The children listed fit well with information given by Emma Jane's daughter, Ethel Mc Master, except that she did not mention Martha.

Luke Waites

#1343
     Luke Waites married Grace Hopkinson Mortimer, daughter of William Mortimer and Betty Hopkinson, on 31 August 1835 at Tong as Nancy which corresponds with Luke's wife in the 1841 census.
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as an agricultural labourer the son-in-law of William Mortimer.1 In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at Birkenshaw Bottoms, Gomersal, as a general labourer aged 45.2
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at Smithies Lane, Gomersal, as a farm labourer.3
In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as the head of household at Brownhill, Batley, as a contractor.4

Children of Luke Waites and Grace Hopkinson Mortimer

  • Caroline Waites b. 1837
  • Hannah Waites b. c 1840
  • George Waites b. c 1842
  • Louisa Waites b. c 1845
  • Emily Waites b. c 1848
  • Sabina Waites b. c 1853
  • Luke Waites b. c 1859

Rosamund Thompson

#1344, b. 1736
Rosamund Thompson|b. 1736|p45.htm#i1344|Thomas Thompson||p299.htm#i8292||||||||||||||||
     Rosamund Thompson was born in 1736. She was the daughter of Thomas Thompson. Rosamund Thompson was baptized on 19 November 1736 at Batley.1
She married William Blakeley, son of Benjamin Blakeley and Sarah Johnson, on 29 December 1756 at Batley Witnesses were John Cowling and Benjamin Healey.1
Rosamund Blakeley married Marmaduke Fox on 31 October 1774 at Batley as Marmaduke Fox of Dewsbury and Rosamund Blakeley of Batley.1

Children of Rosamund Thompson and William Blakeley

  • John Blakeley b. 1757
  • Jonathan Blakeley b. 1760, d. 1761
  • Ann Blakeley b. 1762
  • Aby Blakeley b. 1764
