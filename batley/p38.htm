Thomas Stubley

#1120, b. 1810
Thomas Stubley|b. 1810|p38.htm#i1120|Joseph Stubley|b. 1772\nd. 1824|p33.htm#i1007|Mary Denton||p37.htm#i1119|Abraham Stubley|b. 1737\nd. 1802|p51.htm#i1505|Noah Eastwood|b. 1739\nd. 1792|p51.htm#i1508|Richard Denton||p59.htm#i1743||||

Relationship=2nd cousin 5 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     Thomas Stubley was born in 1810. He was the son of Joseph Stubley and Mary Denton. Thomas Stubley was baptized on 18 September 1810 at Parish Church, Batley.1
He married Betty Ineson, daughter of William Ineson and Rachel Colbeck, on 1 July 1833 at Tong.2
Betty having died in 1836, Thomasa widower on the certificate, married Mary Ann Broadhead, daughter of James Broadhead, on 16 September 1838 at Tong; witnesses were William Ibolderson and William Potter.3 In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at Back Lane, Batley, as a woollen spinner, living next door to his cousin Beaumont Stubley.4
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Ward's Hill, Batley, as a cloth weaver aged 41.5
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at John Ward's Hill, Batley, as a woollen mule spinner.6
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at Albion Street, Batley.7

Child of Thomas Stubley and Betty Ineson

  • Susan Stubley+ b. 6 Jul 1835

Children of Thomas Stubley and Mary Ann Broadhead

  • Joseph Stubley b. 1 Jul 1839
  • Betty Stubley b. 3 May 1843
  • Mary Stubley b. c 1854
  • Ada Stubley b. c 1863
