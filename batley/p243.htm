John McMurchy

#6849, b. 27 January 1861
© image courtesy of Clive Mason
     John McMurchy was born on 27 January 1861. He married Ida M Joy on 13 March 1889.
John McMurchy was an entrepreneur with Hydro dams, a shoe factory, and woollen mills. It was in connection with the latter that he mad visits to Batley where he got to know the Sheards.1
He married Ellen Sheard, daughter of John Sheard and Margaret Maria Hodgson Hogg, on 2 October 1909 at York, Toronto, Canada.
In the census of June 1911 he was listed as the head of household at Brampton, Ontario, Canada, as a manufacturer.2

Children of John McMurchy and Ida M Joy

  • Harry McMurchy b. 3 Jul 1892
  • Roy McMurchy b. 4 Jul 1896
  • Clive McMurchy b. 1903

Child of John McMurchy and Ellen Sheard

  • Gladys Marie McMurchy+ b. 1911, d. Feb 2002

     Gladys Marie McMurchy was born in 1911. She was the daughter of John McMurchy and Ellen Sheard.
In the census of June 1911 she was listed as the daughter of John McMurchy at Brampton, Ontario, Canada.1
In 1914 Gladys Marie McMurchy with her mother, grandmother and half-brother Clive, made a prolonged visit to England, arriving in Liverpool on the "Hesperian" on the 12th of March, and sailing back to Canada on the "Calgarian", arriving in Quebec on the 9th of July. in 1914.2
She was educated at the University of Toronto.
She then worked as a dietician at the Johns Hopkins Hospital, where she was to meet her future husband.3
She married James M Mason III, a surgeon, on 4 July 1936 at Brampton, Ontario.3
Gladys Marie McMurchy died in February 2002.3

Children of Gladys Marie McMurchy and James M Mason III

  • Clive Mason b. 1946
  • Sheard Mason b. 1951
