Charles Bolton

#8041, b. circa 1772, d. 18 September 1839
Charles Bolton|b. c 1772\nd. 18 Sep 1839|p289.htm#i8041|Joseph Bolton|b. 15 Mar 1749|p289.htm#i8043|Lydia Peel|b. 9 Jun 1751|p289.htm#i8050|Joseph Bolton||p289.htm#i8051||||Richard Peel||p289.htm#i8052||||
     Charles Bolton was born circa 1772. He was the son of Joseph Bolton and Lydia Peel.
Charles Bolton married Sarah Saville, daughter of William Saville, on 10 December 1798 at Batley
They were described as as Charles Bolton of this parish and Sarah Saville of the Parish of Birstall. Charles wrote his name, albeit clumsily, with the spelling Boulton, while Sarah made her mark. Witnesses were James Bolland and John Chadwick.1
Charles Bolton died on 18 September 1839 at Upper Batley, Batley; He was described as road man, though had been described as a clothier at the baptisms of his children. John Bolton was present at death, which was attributed to gradual decay.2

Richard Bolton1

#8058, b. 16 April 1791, d. 1867
Richard Bolton|b. 16 Apr 1791\nd. 1867|p289.htm#i8058|Joseph Bolton|b. 15 Mar 1749|p289.htm#i8043|Lydia Peel|b. 9 Jun 1751|p289.htm#i8050|Joseph Bolton||p289.htm#i8051||||Richard Peel||p289.htm#i8052||||
     Richard Bolton was born on 16 April 1791.1 He was the son of Joseph Bolton and Lydia Peel.1 Richard Bolton was baptized on 8 May 1791 at Batley.1
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at Howley, Morley, as a cloth weaver, J (J probably for journeyman and occurs iin some other entries) Richard has living with him his sister Lydia and also Elizabeth and William, children of his brother Charles who had died in 1839, and Nathan, Elizabeth's son.2
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Howley, Morley, as a woollen cloth weaver.3
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at Howley, Morley, as a woollen weaver. His sister Lydia is living with him and there is a lodger, James Thorpe aged 29, a miner.4
He died in 1867. He was buried on 17 February 1867 at Batley ; aged 77 years.1
