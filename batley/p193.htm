Relationship=2nd cousin 3 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     William James Stubley was born on 5 February 1856 at Batley.1 He was the son of Joshua Stubley and Maria Rowley. William James Stubley was baptized on 13 April 1856 at Batley.1
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as a scholar the son of Joshua Stubley at Hume Street, Batley.2
