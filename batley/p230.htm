Charts
Descendants of William Blackburn
     Edward Simpson Roberts was born on 14 September 1912. He was the son of Donald Edward Roberts and Mary Margaret Gaunt. Edward Simpson Roberts was baptized on 22 October 1912 at Centenary Methodist Church, Stanningley.1
He served in the Green Howards, attaining the rank of Captain and, like his father in an earlier war, being awarded the Military Cross between 1939 and 1944.2,3 He died on 1 June 1944 at Anzio, Italy, at age 31.3,2 He was buried in 1944 at Beach Head War Cemetery, Anzio.
