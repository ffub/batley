Patience Sands

#6825, b. 16 June 1803
     Patience Sands was born on 16 June 1803. She was baptized on 7 August 1803 at Batley.1
She married Isaac Hopkinson, son of Isaac Hopkinson and Grace Collison, on 14 October 1827 at Batley They were described as both of this parish at time of marriage. He writes, she makes her mark. Witnesses wereJoseph Chadwick and Charles Ward.1
In the census of 6 June 1841 Patience Hopkinson was listed at Birkenshaw as wife of Isaac Hopkinson.2
In the census of 30 March 1851 Patience Hopkinson was listed at 26 Tong Street, Tong, as wife of Isaac Hopkinson.
In the census of 7 April 1861 Patience Hopkinson was listed at Turnpike Rd, Tong, as wife of Isaac Hopkinson.
