     Joseph Henry Knowles was born in 1839.1 He was the son of Joseph Knowles and Mary Goodall. In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as a scholar the son of Joseph Knowles at Clerk Green, Batley.2
Joseph Henry Knowles married Lucy Hall, daughter of Thomas Hall and Letitia Healey, on 17 September 1864 at Batley Witnesses were Thomas Henry Hall and Elizabeth Blakeley Burnley.3
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at Clerk Green, Batley, as a tanner and carrier employing 6(?) men and 1 boy.4
In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as the head of household at Goose Hill, Batley, as a wool and waste merchant.
