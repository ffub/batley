John Priestley Middlebrook

#3600, b. 1845
John Priestley Middlebrook|b. 1845|p121.htm#i3600|John Middlebrook|b. c 1821|p150.htm#i4446|Eliza Priestley|b. c 1819|p150.htm#i4447|Thomas Middlebrook||p310.htm#i8585||||John Priestley||p310.htm#i8586||||
     John Priestley Middlebrook was born in 1845 at Batley.1 He was the son of John Middlebrook and Eliza Priestley. In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the son of John Middlebrook at Birstall S Street, Birstall.2
John Priestley Middlebrook married Dorothy Brearley, daughter of Robert Brearley and Elizabeth Burnill, on 7 April 1870 at Wesleyan Chapel, Batley.3,4
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at North Terrace, Gomersal, as a salesman in a woollen warehouse.5
In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as the head of household at Hollybank, Field Hill, Batley, as a woollen manufacturer.6
In 1883 John Priestley Middlebrook was Mayor of Batley.
In the census of 5 April 1891 he was listed as the head of household at Purlwell Lane, Ivy Bank, Batley, as a woollen manufacturer aged 46.

Children of John Priestley Middlebrook and Dorothy Brearley

  • Alice Middlebrook b. c 1873
  • Frank Middlebrook b. c 1875
