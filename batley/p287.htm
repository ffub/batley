Susan Ineson

#7985, b. circa 1812
     Susan Ineson was born circa 1812 at Batley. She married William Allott on 26 December 1832 at Dewsbury.1

In the census of 30 March 1851 Susan Wilson was listed at Havercroft, Batley, as wife of George Wilson.3

     Walter Peel Wilson was born in 1870. He was the son of Edward Wilson and Sarah Peel. In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as aged 10 months the son of Edward Wilson at New Road Side, Batley.1
In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as a scholar the son of Edward Wilson at Healey Road, Batley.2
In the census of 5 April 1891 he was listed as a rag grinder. the son of Edward Wilson at 18 Cobden Street, Batley. Walter must have started work in this capacity at around this time, as at his father's death in early 1908 he is said to have worked at The Old Mill for16 years.3
Walter Peel Wilson married Alma Edith Stephenson, daughter of Eli Stephenson, in 1894.4
