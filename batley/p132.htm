#3964, b. 1791
Thomas Clapham|b. 1791|p132.htm#i3964|Thomas Clapham||p54.htm#i1567|Christiana Bawmfirth||p54.htm#i1568|||||||||||||
     Thomas Clapham was born in 1791. He was the son of Thomas Clapham and Christiana Bawmfirth. Thomas Clapham was baptized on 6 March 1791 at Birstall.1
He married Elizabeth Chadwick, daughter of John Chadwick and Elizabeth Gaunt, on 4 November 1813 at Batley They were married by licence, and both write their names. Thomas is described as of Birstall. Witnesses were Ann Hartley and Hannah Smith. I lack absolute proof that this is the right Elizabeth, but the age fits and her brother Jeremiah married Thomas's sister.2 In 1818 Thomas Clapham leased Oakwell Hall at Birstall.3
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Birstall T Street, Gomersal, as a widower aged 60, a maltster employing 1 man.4

John Spurr

#3979, b. circa 1791
     John Spurr was born circa 1791 at Batley. He married Sarah Walker on 19 September 1814 at Batley witnesses Mark Pyrah and William Spedding.1
John Spurr married Rachel Smith, daughter of Richard Smith and Grace Hall, on 17 September 1824 at Batley witnesses Mark Pyrah and Thomas Parr.1
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at Clerk Green, Batley, as a woollen labourer.2
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Clerk Green, Batley, as a clothier, spinner aged 59.3
