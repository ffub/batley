Squire Senior

#3288, b. circa 1800, d. 1867
Squire Senior|b. c 1800\nd. 1867|p111.htm#i3288|Charles Senior||p284.htm#i7931|Mary (?)||p285.htm#i7932|||||||||||||
     Squire Senior was born circa 1800 at Earlsheaton. He was the son of Charles Senior and Mary (?). Squire Senior was baptized on 14 September 1800 at Dewsbury.1
He married Frances Hall, daughter of John Hall and Sarah Gledhill, on 8 April 1822 at Batley.2
Squire Senior was described as of Mirfield at his marriage.2 In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at New Batley as a woollen spinner.3
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Clerk Green, Batley, as a clothier, spinner aged 49. There is a lodger, William Hemingway, aged 13.4
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at Purlwell Lane, Batley, as a woollen weaver. there is a boarder, John Land aged 20, a cloth finisher.
He died in 1867. He was buried on 22 May 1867 at Batley ; age recorded as 66.2
