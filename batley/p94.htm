Jeremiah Shepley

#2829, b. 1626
Jeremiah Shepley|b. 1626|p94.htm#i2829|Richard Shepley|b. 1591\nd. 1631|p96.htm#i2888|Ester Rhodes|b. 1597\nd. 1684|p96.htm#i2896|Thomas Shepley|d. 1609|p97.htm#i2921|Jane Hepworth|b. 5 Apr 1561|p97.htm#i2937|William Rhodes|d. 1631|p97.htm#i2922|Agnes Hall|b. 2 Apr 1564\nd. 1631|p97.htm#i2923|

Relationship=8th great-grandfather of Vivien Eley.
Charts
Ancestry of Vivien Eley Tomlinson
     Jeremiah Shepley was born in 1626 at Mirfield. He was the son of Richard Shepley and Ester Rhodes. Jeremiah Shepley was baptized on 17 August 1626 at Mirfield.1
No Shepleys were listed in Batley on the Protestation returns1642.
He married Grace Birke on 20 December 1658 at Birstall.2
Jeremiah Shepley was listed as having 2 hearths in the Hearth Tax Bill of 1666 at Batley.3
In 1677 Jeremiah Shepley and Joshua Shepley were listed as freeholders at Batley.
Jeremiah Shepley received a bequest of 3s in the will of Joshua Shepley on 16 April 1679.
Jeremiah Shepley may also have had a daughter Elizabeth. An Elizabeth Shepley married John Stead of Tong at Mirfield on July 15th 1694, and a John Stead is mentioned as one of Jeremiah's heirs:-

"Wee doe also finde John Stead heire to Jeremy Shepley Land, who hath now done his ffealty (the same is in right of his wife)."4,1

Children of Jeremiah Shepley and Grace Birke

  • Marmaduke Shepley+ b. 1660, d. 1722
  • Jeremy Shepley+ b. c 1667, d. 5 Apr 1746

Jane Raynor

#2837, b. circa 1684, d. 19 December 1748
     Jane Raynor was born circa 1684; from age at death. I think it probable that Jane, rather older than her husband, was a widow when she married Jeremiah Shepley, and that the two daughters -in-law, Jane Reyner and Mary Hodgson who her husband mentions in his will were her daughters by a previous marriage to a Reyner.
She transcribed as Jeremy Shepherd of Batley married Jeremiah Shepley of Scholecroft, son of Marmaduke Shepley and Anne Eton, on 18 August 1725 at St. Peter's, Leeds,
by licence. Jane Shepley died on 19 December 1748; in the 65th year of her age.1

  • Ann Reyner
