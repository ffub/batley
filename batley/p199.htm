Relationship=4th cousin 3 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     Blakeley Greenwood was born circa 1848. He was the son of Richard Greenwood and Sarah Tidder. In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as a scholar the son of Richard Greenwood at Providence Street, Batley.1
He was listed in the census of 2 April 1871 at Commercial Street, Batley, as a draper's assistant in the household of Eli Ibberson; a Draper and Town Councillor. Blakeley Greenwood is employed as an assistant, and there are an apprentice, two milliners and a general servant.2 Blakeley Greenwood died on 3 August 1884 at Batley; described as draper of Park Road, Batley.3 He was buried on 5 August 1884 at The Cemetery, Batley.3
