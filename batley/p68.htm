Charts
Descendants of Michael Sheard born 1688
     James Sheard was born on 15 July 1809 at Batley. He was the son of Jonas Sheard and Eleanor Hey. James Sheard was baptized on 15 September 1809 at Batley.1

James Sheard married Ann Colbeck, daughter of William Colbeck and Elizabeth Richardson, on 30 August 1830 at Batley They were married on the same day as Elizabeth Colbeck and Joseph Senior. Witnesses were the same for both marriages, Thos Wilby and Will Mayman.1
In the 6 June 1841 census he was listed as the head of household at Commercial St, Batley, as an Innkeeper. This was of the Wilton Arms, said by Willans to have been built by one Peter Heptonstall,
"but afterwards owned and kept by Mr. James Sheard, a noted musician and leader of the Batley Brass Band, also of the Up Lane Scholars at their anniversaries."2,3

James Sheard left a will in 1841 which put money in trust to provide for his wife and children, the trustees being his friends George Newsome of Up Lane, clothier, and Joseph Jubb the Younger, clothier.
James Sheard died in November 1841 at Batley at age 32.
He was buried on 11 November 1841 at Batley.1
