James Newsome Poole

#5674, b. 18 October 1855, d. 1925

Descendants of Michael Sheard born 1688
     James Newsome Poole was born on 18 October 1855 at Batley.1 He was the son of William Poole and Mary Burnley. James Newsome Poole was baptized on 16 December 1855 at Batley.1
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as aged 5 the son of an unknown person at Billy Well Lane, Batley.2
He married Sarah Kitson, daughter of Timothy Kitson and Elizabeth Day, in 1877.3
In the census of 1892 he was listed as the head of household at Little Falls, Herkimer, New Yok, as a mill hand.4
In the census of 5 June 1900 he was listed as the head of household at Little Falls, Herkimer, New York, as a spinner.5
In the census of 1910 he was listed as the head of household at Little Falls City, Herkimer, New York, as foreman, spinning room.6
James Newsome Poole died in 1925.

Children of James Newsome Poole and Sarah Kitson

  • Day Kitson Poole b. 1877
  • Mary E Poole b. c 1879
  • George E Poole b. 1880
  • Elsie Poole b. c 1894
