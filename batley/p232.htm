John Whitaker

#6557, b. 1817
John Whitaker|b. 1817|p232.htm#i6557|John Whitaker||p231.htm#i6553|Mary Senior|b. 1778|p231.htm#i6554|Joshua Whitaker||p232.htm#i6555||||||||||
     John Whitaker was born in 1817. He was the son of John Whitaker and Mary Senior. John Whitaker was baptized on 15 June 1817 at Batley.1
John Whitaker was said to have been apprenticed to Michael Spedding.2
John Whitaker married Mary Simpson, daughter of John Simpson, on 6 November 1837 at Batley Witnesses were Benjamin Senior and Joseph Hudswell.1
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at Havercroft Lane End, Batley, as a woollen manufacturer.3
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Havercroft, Batley, as a woollen manufacturer employing 8 women, 13 girls, 34 men and 16 boys.4
In 1858 John Whitaker was bankrupt.
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at Albion Street, Batley,and back in business as a woollen rag merchant employing 8 (?) hands.5

  • Benjamin Whitaker b. 1839
  • Isabella Whitaker b. 1841
  • Martha Whitaker b. c 1843
  • Elizabeth Whitaker b. c 1845
  • John Whitaker b. c 1848
