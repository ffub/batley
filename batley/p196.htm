Thomas Bailey

#5623, b. circa 1806
     Thomas Bailey was born circa 1806 at Soothill. He married Susey Castle on 6 December 1829 at Dewsbury.1
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Hanging Heaton as a widower, a master cordwainer.2
Thomas Bailey appeared as a widower, a cordwainer, on the census of 7 April 1861 in the household of Samuel Mitchell and Ellen Mitchell at Hanging Heaton.3

Children of Thomas Bailey and Susey Castle

  • Charles Bailey+ b. 9 Mar 1830, d. 12 Oct 1924
  • Ellen Bailey+ b. 1834
