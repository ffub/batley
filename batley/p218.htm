Thomas Wilby Lee

#6196, b. 21 December 1788, d. 21 September 1863
Thomas Wilby Lee|b. 21 Dec 1788\nd. 21 Sep 1863|p218.htm#i6196|Isaac Lee|b. 1753|p246.htm#i6948|Elizabeth Wilby||p246.htm#i6949|||||||||||||
     Thomas Wilby Lee was born in 1788. He was baptized on 21 December 1788 at Birstall.1
He was the son of Isaac Lee and Elizabeth Wilby. Thomas Wilby Lee married Jane Shotton on 23 September 1821 at Wakefield.2
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at Batley New Mill, Batley, as bookkeeper. This I believe refers to Hick Lane Mill, and Thomas to be the "Tommy Lee" referred to by Isaac Binns as bookkeeper there in his youth.3
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Havercroft, Batley, as a manager and bookeeper.4
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at Pally Burnley Fold, Batley, as a retired bookkeeper.5
Thomas Wilby Lee died on 21 September 1863 at age 74. He was buried on 23 September 1863 at Batley ; aged 76, of Dewsbury.6

Ann Brook||p218.htm#i6209|William Brook||p241.htm#i6820||||||||||||||||
     Ann Brook is the daughter of William Brook. Ann Brook married Joseph Preston, son of Joshua Preston, on 22 August 1799 at Batley.1
In the census of 6 June 1841 Ann Preston was listed at Purlwell, Batley, as wife of Joseph Preston.2

Children of Ann Brook and Joseph Preston

  • Joshua Preston b. 28 Feb 1800
  • Joseph Preston b. 9 Sep 1802
  • John Preston b. 23 Nov 1805
  • Benjamin Preston+ b. 1814
