John Preston

#4373, b. circa 1816
John Preston|b. c 1816|p148.htm#i4373|William Preston||p288.htm#i8031||||||||||||||||
     John Preston was born circa 1816 at Dewsbury. He was the son of William Preston. John Preston married Susanna Thompson, daughter of John Thompson and Rachel Colbeck, on 17 September 1844 at BatleyThis marriage deduced to be correct one from presence of Susanna's sister Rachel A Thompson with the couple in 1861.1 In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as the head of household at Carlinghow, Batley, as a woollen spinner.2
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at Carlinghow Road, Batley, as a woollen cloth weaver.3
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at High St, Batley, as a clothier.4

John Pyrah

#4377, b. 29 March 1812, d. 27 January 1895
John Pyrah|b. 29 Mar 1812\nd. 27 Jan 1895|p148.htm#i4377|Mark Pyrah|b. 1781\nd. 23 Mar 1852|p60.htm#i1770|Elizabeth Hardcastle|b. 8 Dec 1778|p135.htm#i4041|Joseph Pyrah|b. 1756\nd. 25 Apr 1806|p48.htm#i1429|Elizabeth Gledhill|b. 1760\nd. 5 Oct 1825|p57.htm#i1669|John Hardcastle||p284.htm#i7922|Jane Preston||p284.htm#i7923|

Relationship=2nd cousin 5 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     John Pyrah was born on 29 March 1812 at Batley.1 He was the son of Mark Pyrah and Elizabeth Hardcastle. John Pyrah was baptized on 18 September 1812 at Batley.1
In the census of 30 March 1851 he was listed as a book keeper, unmarried, aed 38 the son of Mark Pyrah at Clerk Green, Batley.2
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at Clerk Green, Batley, as a bookkeeper. Living with him are niece Ann, daughter of his brother Joseph, as housekeeper, and nephew John, son of his brother Jeremiah.3
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at Dark Lane, Batley, as an overlooker.4
This must be the John Pyrah described as bookkeeper to the Firm of Brearley Hall & Co, of Purlwell Mill, at the dissolution of that firm in 1873.5 In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as the head of household at Crescent St, Batley, as a bookkeeper, unemployed.6
He died on 27 January 1895 at age 82; aged 82 years.7

William Blakeley

#4385

Relationship=9th great-grandfather of Vivien Eley.
Charts
Ancestry of Vivien Eley Tomlinson
     William Blakeley married Jane Hall, daughter of John Hall and Jane Walker, on 8 September 1626 at Hartshead.1
In 1642 William Blakeley was listed on the Protestation Return for Hartshead.
He made a will on 18 April 1656. His wife was to have a third part of his goods as was customary at the time, and the younger children were to have ten pounds each when they were 21, or in the case of the younger boys when they had finished their apprenticeships. Eldest son William was to have his best pair of draught animals and half of his wains and ploughs. Althoough we know little of the man, I cannot help but feel a fondness for one who called his best oxen lambe and dareling.
