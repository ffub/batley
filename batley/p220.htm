Samuel Preston

#6253, b. 1815, d. circa 1865
Samuel Preston|b. 1815\nd. c 1865|p220.htm#i6253|Joseph Preston||p220.htm#i6257|Jane Speight||p283.htm#i7906|||||||||||||
     Samuel Preston was born in 1815. He was the son of Joseph Preston and Jane Speight. Samuel Preston was baptized on 17 September 1815 at Batley.1,2
He married Hannah Gomersall, daughter of William Gomersall and Esther Bruce, on 12 November 1846 at Rehoboth Chapel independents, Dewsbury Union, Both made their marks. Samuel was listed as a joiner of Clerk Green; son of Joseph, also a joiner. Witnesses wereJohn Newsome and John Webster.1
In 1856 Samuel Preston and Joseph Preston emigrated to Australiataking an unasisted passage on the "Maggie Miller"with his brother.1 Samuel Preston died circa 1865; He is presumed to have died in Australia.1

Children of Samuel Preston and Hannah Gomersall

  • Joseph Preston b. c 1849
  • Benjamin Preston b. 1859
  • Esther Jane Preston b. 1862

#6254, b. circa 1849

     Joseph Preston was born circa 1849 at Batley.
He was the son of Samuel Preston and Hannah Gomersall. Joseph Preston and her son Hannah emigrated in 1858 to Australia to join Samuel, taking an assisted passage on the ship "Caractacus."1
Joseph returned to Batley following his father's death.
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as as a cloth finisher aged 22 the step-son of Charles Sheard at Ward's Hill, Batley.2
Joseph Preston married Elizabeth Blackburn, daughter of George Blackburn and Martha (?), on 11 November 1871.1
Joseph Preston married Lucy Ayre in 1884.1
