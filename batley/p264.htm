Samuel Mitchell

#7401, b. circa 1829
     Samuel Mitchell was born circa 1829 at Soothill. He married Ellen Bailey, daughter of Thomas Bailey and Susey Castle, in 1853.1
In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as the head of household at Hanging Heaton as book keeper and manager of a woollen mill.2
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as the head of household at Watson's Buildings, Soothill, as a book keeper.3
In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as the head of household at Mill St West, Thornhill, as a book keeper.4
When Samuel and Ellen Mitchell held a large reception to celebrate their Silver Wedding in 1914, Emily Simpson and George Simpson, their daughter and son-in-law were present on a visit from America.5
