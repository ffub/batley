Ann Briggs

#6297, b. circa 1841, d. 1902
Ann Briggs|b. c 1841\nd. 1902|p221.htm#i6297|Abraham Briggs||p314.htm#i8688||||||||||||||||
     Ann Briggs was born circa 1841 at Batley. She was the daughter of Abraham Briggs. Ann Briggs married Robert Brearley, son of George Brearley and Harriot Hey, on 25 December 1863 at Batley.1
In the census of 2 April 1871 Ann Brearley was listed at Carlinghow Lane, Batley, as a dressmaker, wife of Robert Brearley.2
In the census of 3 April 1881 Ann Brearley was listed at Carlinghow Lane, Batley, as wife of Robert Brearley.3
In the census of 5 April 1891 she was listed as the head of household at 2 Pynate Terrace, off Carlinghow Lane, Batley, as housekeeper. Robert is not at home, but also present is a nephew, Herbert Brearley aged 25.4
In the census of 31 March 1901 Ann Brearley was listed at South Bank Road, Batley, as wife of Robert Brearley.5
Ann Briggs died in 1902 at Batley. She was buried on 26 December 1902 at Batley Cemetery.6

Child of Ann Briggs and Robert Brearley

  • Joseph Brearley b. c 1865
