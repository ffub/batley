Hannah Lister

#7675, b. circa 1852
Hannah Lister|b. c 1852|p275.htm#i7675|John Lister|b. c 1826|p275.htm#i7676|Harriet (?)||p288.htm#i8014|||||||||||||
     Hannah Lister was born circa 1852. She was the daughter of John Lister and Harriet (?). Hannah Lister married Charles Edward Bolton, son of Joseph Bolton and Lucy Firth, on 17 January 1874 at The Register Office, Dewsbury, Witnesses were John Joseph Smithson and Mary Sheard. Charles was described at the time as an engine tenter in a woollen mill, of Mill Lane, Batley, and Hannah as a spinster of Providence St.1
In the census of 3 April 1881 she was listed as a woollen power loom weaver the daughter of Harriet Lister at Providence Street, Batley. Her husband was not present.2

Charles Clackner

#7679, b. 1859
     Charles Clackner was born in 1859 at New York. He married Emma Lincoln Bailey, daughter of Charles Bailey and Ann Cynthia Brooks, circa 1892.
In the census of 6 June 1900 he was listed as the head of household at West Main Street, Little Falls, Herkimer, New York, as a mill operative.1
In the census of 18 April 1910 he was listed as the head of household at Minden, Montgomery, New York, as a painter in a carriage shop.2
In the census of 16 January 1920 he was listed as the head of household at German Flatts, Herkimer, New York, as an inspector in the typewriter industry.3
Charles Clackner was a witness when Charles Bailey was made several trips back to England, and perhaps the last was in 1922, aged 92, when in 1922.4 In the census of 8 April 1930 he was listed as the head of household at 21 West Clark Street, German Flatts, Herkimer, New York, as of no occupation. There is a lodger, Anna Jefferson aged 57 born in Germany.5

Child of Charles Clackner and Emma Lincoln Bailey

  • Bailey Clackner b. Oct 1892
