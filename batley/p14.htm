Abraham Ibberson married Ann Walker, daughter of Abraham Sunderland, on 7 April 1793 at Batley.1
Abraham Ibberson married Mary Richardson on 2 August 1804 at Batley by licence, witnesses Benjamin Law and ? Richardson.1
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at Havercroft, Batley, as a farmer aged 70.2
Abraham Ibberson died on 4 April 1848 at Batley; aged 80.1
