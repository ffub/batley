Sam Sykes

#8366, b. circa 1870
     Sam Sykes was born circa 1870. He married Ada Sheard, daughter of Samuel Sheard and Agnes Sheard, on 5 October 1893 at Batley.1
In the census of 31 March 1901 he was listed as the head of household at 84 Dark Lane, Batley, as a foreman letter-press printer.2

Children of Sam Sykes and Ada Sheard

  • Leonard Sheard Sykes b. c 1898
  • Dorothy Sykes b. 6 Aug 1899
