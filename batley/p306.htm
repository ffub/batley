Relationship=1st cousin 3 times removed of Vivien Eley.
     Christopher Tinker Armytage Robinson was born circa 1854 at Sheffield. He was the son of James Robinson and Elizabeth Ann (?). In the census of 7 April 1861 he was listed as aged 6 the son of James Robinson at Sheffield.1
In the census of 2 April 1871 he was listed as a stone mason the son of Elizabeth Ann Robinson at Alma Terrace, Batley.2
Christopher Tinker Armytage Robinson married Fanny Harrison, daughter of Thomas Harrison and Ellen Burnley, on 27 December 1875 at Batley Witnesses were James Smith and Edward Richardson.3
In the census of 3 April 1881 he was listed as the head of household at Dark Lane, Batley, as a stone mason.4
In the census of 5 April 1891 he was listed as the head of household at 7 Beck Lane, Batley, as a stone mason.5
In the census of 31 March 1901 he was listed as the head of household at 47 Clerk Green, Batley, as Inspector of Public Works.6
Christopher Tinker Armytage Robinson died on 28 June 1915.7
