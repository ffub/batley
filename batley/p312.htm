James Gunnis

#8623, b. 1771
James Gunnis|b. 1771|p312.htm#i8623|Joseph Gunnis||p312.htm#i8620|Ann (?)||p312.htm#i8621|||||||||||||
     James Gunnis was born in 1771. He was the son of Joseph Gunnis and Ann (?). James Gunnis was baptized on 14 July 1771 at Combe, Oxfordshire.1
He married Mary (?).
On 22 June 1808 Benjamin Law pulled out of a partnership with 2 other Batley manufacturers, George Newsome and John Sheard and James Gunnis.2
In the census of 6 June 1841 he was listed as the head of household at Hart St, St Alphage Sion College, Middlesex, as a warehouseman. With him were James Willans from Batley and James's wife Mary, who I believe to be James Gunnis's niece. Also present were a William Horsley, whose occupation looks like seedsman, and Joseph Knowles, a blanket manufacturer. None of these people were born locally, and although I have not been able to identify this Joseph Knowles it will be noted that it is a surname to be found in the Batley aea.3
